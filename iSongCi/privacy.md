<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam built iSongCi (爱宋词 / 愛宋詞).

## Summary

**Data Not Collected.** iSongCi makes **zero outbound network requests** after install. **No account. No ads. No analytics SDK.** All ci data is stored locally. Optional **iCloud Sync** for favorites, notes, appearance, reading streak, and tab visibility uses **your private iCloud** only.

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Ci database | 21,592 bundled Song lyrics |
| Favorites | Works you star |
| Private notes | Per-poem notes (only you can see) |
| Reading streak | Daily reading check-in & total count |
| Preferences | Theme, accent color, reading layout, font scale, tab visibility |
| Notification settings | Daily ci reminder (local scheduling only) |

## iCloud Sync

Optional sync through iCloud Key-Value Store for: favorites, notes, appearance (light/dark/system), body font scale, category/favorites tab visibility, and reading streak. **Ci text is never uploaded** — it always stays in the app bundle.

## Permissions

| Permission | When | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Notifications | Daily ci reminder | Local scheduling only — no server |
| iCloud | Optional sync | Sync favorites, notes & preferences |
| Photo Library (Add) | Saving share cards | Write-only — save ci card images to Photos |

## In-App Purchase

Optional tip jar via Apple StoreKit (`com.iHTCboy.iSongCi.6`). Payment is handled entirely by Apple; we do not receive payment details directly.

## Contact

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
