<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iSongCi (爱宋词) is a **vertical Song Ci (宋词) studio** — focused on *ci* lyrics, *cipai* tune patterns, and Song-dynasty poets, not a generic poetry catalog. **No account required.** All **21,592 ci works** are bundled on your device: **zero network requests** after install.

**At a glance:** 21,592 ci · 1,509 authors · 1,461 cipai · plain translations · daily ci with local reminders · continuous reading & playlists · 13 share-card styles · 5 widgets · Siri · optional iCloud · **no ads, no tracking**.

## Getting Started

1. Open iSongCi and follow the short onboarding (or skip).
2. On **Home**, open **Today's Ci** or browse by author / cipai / collection.
3. In a work detail, use immersive reading, TTS, share cards, favorites, and playlists.
4. Optional: enable **daily reminder**, add **widgets**, or try **Siri / Shortcuts** (`isongci://`).

> **Full step-by-step guide:** every section is preserved in the [**FAQ / User Guide**](faq) — first launch, browsing, reading, widgets, Siri, notifications, settings, and more.
