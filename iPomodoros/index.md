**Stop fighting the clock. Start finishing what matters.**

**iPomodoro** (爱番茄 / 愛番茄) is a native SwiftUI **focus studio** for **iPhone & iPad** — built around the timeless [Pomodoro Technique](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomodoro_Technique): work in rhythm, rest with intention, and turn scattered minutes into measurable progress.

Five thoughtfully designed tabs — **Pomodoro**, **Study Timer**, **Countdown Tasks**, **Statistics**, and **Settings** — in one calm, native app. Gorgeous H:M:S typography, **1000+ on-device motivation quotes**, **dual Live Activities** on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island, **five widget families**, and a **Statistics dashboard** that transforms focus into streaks, heatmaps, goals, and share-worthy report cards. Jump in via **Home Screen Quick Actions**, **`iPomodoro://` URLs**, or **Siri**; read the in-app **App Guide** anytime.

**No account. No analytics. Your focus stays on your device.** Optional **iCloud sync** for tasks and/or study sessions — separate toggles, your Apple ID via CloudKit, not our servers.

> **Ready in seconds.** Long-press the Home Screen icon, ask Siri *"Open Pomodoro in iPomodoro"*, or glance at a widget — and you're already in focus mode.

## Why iPomodoro?

Most timer apps just count down. iPomodoro is built for people who **actually want to finish things**:

- **Two-tap start** — sensible defaults out of the box; tap the big digits when you need custom durations.
- **Tasks that pull you forward** — link a countdown deadline to every session; long-press any task → **Focus with Pomodoro** or **Focus with Study Timer**.
- **Progress you can feel** — daily goals, monthly heatmaps, week-over-week comparisons, and **9 achievement badges** with full-screen unlock celebrations.
- **Share the proof** — branded dashboard long cards, period reports, year-in-review slides, and App Store QR snapshots — perfect for study groups or personal milestones.
- **Always in sight** — separate Pomodoro & Study Timer widgets, **Today's Stats** at a glance, Live Activity countdowns, and motivational quotes on your Lock Screen.
- **Private by design** — timers, stats, and quotes stay on your device; optional iCloud uses Apple CloudKit, not our servers.
- **Siri & Shortcuts native** — open any tab, or ask *"How long did I focus today in iPomodoro?"* without opening a spreadsheet.

## Getting Started

1. Open the **Pomodoro** tab. Tap the timer digits to set work duration, or use the defaults (25 min work · 5 min short break · 15 min long break).
2. Tap **Start** and focus. When a session ends, the app switches to break automatically — or notifies you if the app is in the background.
3. Switch to **Study Timer** for free-form sessions: set any hour/minute length and run without Pomodoro cycles.
4. Open **Tasks** to add countdown goals — pin important ones, drag to reorder, and optionally enable **Sync tasks with iCloud** under the gear icon.
5. Open **Statistics** for the focus dashboard — today's overview, goals, heatmap, achievements, and optional **iCloud sync** for sessions.
6. Add **Home Screen** or **Lock Screen** widgets — separate **Pomodoro** and **Study Timer** timers, **Countdown Tasks**, **Today's Stats**, and optional **Daily Motivation**. See **Settings → Widgets → How to Use**.
7. Enable **Live Activity** independently in each timer mode (**gear icon → Live Activity**).
8. Customize durations, sounds, and **page themes** from each tab's **gear icon** (including the **Statistics** tab's own red accent) — timer settings moved out of the global Settings tab for faster access.
9. **Long-press the app icon** on the Home Screen for quick actions (Pomodoro, Study Timer, Tasks, Statistics — customize up to 4 in **Settings → App settings → Home Screen shortcuts**). Open the same destinations via **`iPomodoro://`** URLs — see the [URL Scheme Reference](url-scheme). Ask Siri: *"How long did I focus today in iPomodoro?"*
10. Read the in-app **App Guide** under **Settings → App settings → App Guide** for workflows, widgets, and shortcuts.

## Focus Modes

### Pomodoro — Classic focus cycles

The heart of the app. Work intervals, short breaks, and long breaks rotate automatically based on your settings.

- **Link a countdown task** — optional **Link task** button records which deadline you're working toward; shows up in Statistics breakdown.

- **Fully customizable durations** — work hours/minutes, short break, long break, and long-break interval (every N pomodoros).
- **Tap-to-edit timer** — tap the large H:M:S display to open a duration picker; stepper rows in settings for fine tuning.
- **Chakra Petch digit display** — stable, jitter-free countdown typography scaled from the original Flutter design.
- **Startup motivation** — a full-screen quote appears once per app launch when you enter the Pomodoro tab (never spammy, never repeated on tab switch).
- **After abandoning a session** — a full-screen quote can appear when you give up mid-timer (same beautiful UI as startup).
- **Completion feedback** — haptics, optional alarm sound, and a Live Activity **completed** state that dismisses after 10 seconds.
- **Confirm phase completion** (optional) — when enabled, a dialog appears after each work or break segment before the next phase starts; when off, the next phase begins automatically after the alarm.
- **Keep screen awake** — optional always-on display while a session is running.
- **Focus Mode reminder** (optional) — when you start a timer, iPomodoro can remind you to turn on iOS Focus if it is off; the alert can open **Settings**, but apps cannot enable Focus for you.

### Study Timer — Learn at your own pace

Not every session fits a 25-minute box.

- Set **any duration** from hours down to minutes.
- **Link a countdown task** — same optional **Link task** button as Pomodoro.
- Same polished timer UI, tick sounds, background notifications, and Live Activity support as Pomodoro.
- Independent theme colors — default ocean blue gradient, fully customizable.
- **Focus Mode reminder** (optional) — same as Pomodoro; prompts you to enable iOS Focus when starting a session.

### Countdown Tasks — Deadlines with clarity

Track what matters and see exactly how many days remain.

- **Pinned section** — pin key deadlines; they stay at the top.
- **Custom order** — drag to reorder; sorting switches to custom automatically.
- **Sort menu** — by date, modified time, or your manual order (toolbar or task settings).
- **Optional iCloud sync** — Tasks tab → gear → **Sync tasks with iCloud**; Statistics settings → **Sync study statistics with iCloud** (independent toggles).
- **Link a task while focusing** — pick a countdown task on the timer screen, or use **Focus with Pomodoro** / **Focus with Study Timer** from a task's context menu.
- **Swipe to edit, pin, or delete**; add from the navigation bar. When editing, pick one of **9 accent colors** for the task (shown in the list and Tasks widget).
- **Due date reminders** — local notification at your exact target datetime (toggle only appears when the target is still in the future).
- **Reminder manager** — Tasks → gear → **Task due reminders**; grouped by Today / Tomorrow / Next 7 days / Later; tap a row to edit or delete from the sheet footer.

### Study Statistics — See your progress

Dedicated **Statistics** dashboard with Swift Charts and its **own page theme** (gradient background + title bar). Toolbar **share** (dashboard long card) + **gear** for settings.

- **Today at a glance** — focus time, pomodoros, completion rate, current/longest streak.
- **Daily goals** — pomodoro count, focus minutes, weekly focus target with progress rings (toggle in settings).
- **Week-over-week comparison**, **monthly heatmap** (prev/next month, tap month title to jump via year/month picker, **Back to this month**; **future dates** shown as dashed-outline placeholders), **achievements** (9 badges — **unlock celebration overlay** on the Statistics tab, tap for detail, share list or single badge).
- Filter by **All / Pomodoro / Study Timer** and **Today / Week / Month / Year / All time / Custom date range**.
- **Weekday & hour distributions** (weekday labels follow your app language), **breakdown by linked task**, trend chart, recent sessions (swipe to delete).
- **Period report** & **year-in-review** screens with image share; **Stats Glance** home-screen widget for today's progress.

**Statistics settings** (gear):

| Group | Options |
|-------|---------|
| **Theme** | Title bar & background gradient (Statistics tab accent) |
| **Goals** | Enable goals, show on dashboard, daily/weekly targets |
| **iCloud** | Optional study-session sync (CloudKit `StudySession`, plus `StatsGoals` & `StatsAchievements` for goals and badge state) |
| **Achievements** | View unlocked badges |
| **Counting / Display / Data** | Exclude abandoned, minimum duration, remember filters, **custom date range**, CSV import/export, retention, clear all |

**Share as image:** Branded cards with App Store QR — app promotion (Settings), **Statistics dashboard long card** (toolbar; captures current filters, summary, heatmap, distributions, and trends), period reports, year-in-review, **achievements list or single badge**. Statistics share cards follow your **Statistics tab page theme** colors.

CSV columns: `kind,started_at,ended_at,duration_seconds,outcome` plus optional `task_id,task_title,cloud_id`. Data stays on-device by default; enable **Sync study statistics with iCloud** for cross-device merge under your Apple ID.

## Live Activity & Dynamic Island

Available on supported iPhones (iOS 16.2+). **Pomodoro and Study Timer each have their own Live Activity** — enable them separately under each tab's gear settings.

| State | What you see |
|-------|----------------|
| **Running** | Live countdown in the current mode's accent color |
| **Paused** | Static remaining time |
| **Completed** | Celebration message, auto-dismiss after 10 seconds |
| **Background hint** | Today's motivation quote as a single-line hint while counting down |

Compact Dynamic Island layout: countdown on the left, mode icon on the right — minimal width, maximum clarity.

**Session recovery:** If iOS terminates the app while your timer still shows on the Lock Screen, iPomodoro restores the in-progress session when you reopen it and jumps to the active Pomodoro or Study tab automatically — **unless** you launched via a **Home Screen Quick Action**, which takes priority over the auto tab switch.

## Widgets

All widgets read on-device shared state via App Group — no network required.

| Widget | Sizes | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **iPomodoro · Pomodoro** | Small, Medium, Lock Screen (rectangular & inline) | Pomodoro tab timer only — updates when you start a session in the Pomodoro tab |
| **iPomodoro · Study Timer** | Small, Medium, Lock Screen (rectangular & inline) | Study tab timer only — independent from the Pomodoro widget |
| **Countdown Tasks** | Small, Medium, Large | Upcoming pinned tasks with days remaining; **each row uses the task's accent color** |

| **Daily Motivation** | Small, Medium, Large | One deterministic daily quote — synced with in-app quote language settings |

The motivation quote algorithm is `(year × 400 + dayOfYear) % count`, so everyone sees a stable "quote of the day" that changes cleanly at day boundary.

**Widget appearance:** Settings tab → **Widgets** → toggle **Widget background follows theme** to mirror your global accent gradient, or use a clean white / system dark background while keeping accent colors on titles.

**Need help?** Settings → Widgets → **How to Use** covers Home Screen, Lock Screen, StandBy, **Pomodoro / Study Timer / Today's Stats** widget gallery, Live Activity, and FAQ — including migrating from the older single timer widget.

## Privacy & Data

- **Timers and settings** — stored locally in UserDefaults on your device.
- **Tasks** — stored locally in SQLite; optional sync to **your iCloud account** via Apple CloudKit when you turn it on.
- **Study statistics** — session history in on-device SQLite; optional **iCloud sync** for sessions, **daily goals**, and **achievement unlock/celebration state** (Statistics → gear, separate from task sync). Uses incremental CloudKit sync when enabled.
- **Widgets & Live Activity** — Pomodoro / Study / Tasks / **Today's Stats** / motivation snapshots via App Group (`group.com.iHTCboy.iPomodoros`); stays on your device except optional iCloud sync you control.
- **No analytics SDKs**, no advertising, no third-party tracking.
- **No iPomodoro account** — ever.
- Optional in-app support purchase is handled entirely by **Apple StoreKit**.

## 快速上手

1. 打开 **番茄** Tab，点按大号计时数字设置工作时长，或直接使用默认（25 分钟工作 · 5 分钟短休息 · 15 分钟长休息）。
2. 点 **开始** 专注。一段结束后自动进入休息；若 App 在后台，会推送通知提醒你。
3. 切换到 **计时** Tab，自由设定任意时长，不受番茄循环约束。
4. 在 **定任务** 中添加倒数目标——**置顶**重要任务、**拖拽排序**；可在齿轮中开启 **通过 iCloud 同步定任务**。
5. 打开 **统计** Tab — 今日概览、目标、热力图、成就；可在齿轮中可选 **通过 iCloud 同步学习统计**。
6. 添加 **主屏 / 锁屏小组件** — **番茄钟**、**学习计时**、**定任务**、**今日统计** 与 **今日励志**。详见 **设置 → 小组件 → 使用教程**。
7. 在各计时模式 **齿轮 → Live Activity** 中**分别**开启实时活动。
8. 在各 Tab **齿轮** 中调整时长、声音与 **页面主题**（**统计 Tab** 可独立设置专注红主题）——计时相关设置已从全局设置页拆出，就近配置更方便。
9. **长按主屏图标** 可快速打开番茄/计时/定任务/统计等（**设置 → 应用设置 → 主屏快捷操作**，最多 4 项）。亦可用 **`iPomodoro://`** URL 打开相同入口——详见 [URL Scheme 说明](url-scheme)。可对 Siri 说：*「爱番茄今天专注了多久？」*
10. 阅读 **设置 → 应用设置 → 应用指南**，了解工作流、小组件与快捷入口。

## 专注模式

### 番茄学习 — 经典专注循环

- **关联定任务** — 可选 **关联任务** 按钮，记录本次专注对应的倒数日；统计页可按任务拆分。
- **时长完全可配** — 工作时分、短休息、长休息、长休息间隔（每 N 个番茄）。
- **点按编辑计时** — 点击 H:M:S 大数字打开滚轮选择器；设置页 Stepper 微调。
- **Chakra Petch 数字显示** — 稳定无抖动的倒计时排版，比例继承自 Flutter 原版。
- **启动励志** — 进入番茄 Tab 时全屏展示一句语录（每次启动仅一次，切换 Tab 不重复打扰）。
- **放弃计时后** — 中途放弃会话时也可全屏展示励志语录（与启动弹窗同款 UI）。
- **完成反馈** — 触感、可选闹钟音、Live Activity **完成态**（10 秒后自动消失）。
- **阶段完成确认**（可选）——开启后每段工作/休息结束弹窗确认再进下一阶段；关闭则闹钟后自动衔接。
- **防息屏** — 计时进行中可选保持屏幕常亮。
- **专注模式联动**（可选）——开始计时时若系统专注模式未开启，会弹窗提醒；可跳转系统设置手动开启（App 无法代开 Focus）。

### 计时学习 — 按你的节奏学

- 任意 **小时 + 分钟** 时长。
- **关联定任务** — 与番茄模式相同的 **关联任务** 按钮。
- 与番茄模式共享精致 UI、滴答声、后台通知与 Live Activity。
- 独立主题色——默认海洋蓝渐变，可完全自定义。
- **专注模式联动**（可选）——与番茄模式相同，开始计时时可提醒开启系统 Focus。

### 定任务 — 截止日期，一目了然

- **置顶区** — 置顶关键倒数日，始终显示在列表顶部。
- **自定义顺序** — 拖拽排序；自动切换为「自定义顺序」模式。
- **排序 Menu** — 按日期、修改时间或手动顺序（导航栏或任务设置）。
- **可选 iCloud 同步** — 定任务 → 齿轮 → **通过 iCloud 同步定任务**；统计 → 齿轮 → **通过 iCloud 同步学习统计**（**独立开关**）。
- **关联任务专注** — 计时屏选择关联任务；定任务 **长按菜单 → 番茄专注 / 计时专注** 跳转对应 Tab 并绑定。
- **左滑编辑 / 置顶 / 删除**；导航栏添加。编辑时可从 **9 种 accent 色** 中为任务选色（列表与定任务 Widget 同步显示）。

### 学习统计 — 看见进步

- 范围/周期筛选（含 **自定义日期范围**）；**星期与时段分布**（星期标签随 App 语言）、**按关联任务拆分**、趋势图、最近列表（左滑删除）。

**分享图片**：应用推广卡（设置）、**统计 Tab 仪表盘长图**（含当前筛选下的汇总、热力图、分布与趋势）、周期报告、年度回顾、**成就列表/单枚**（含 App Store 二维码）；统计类分享图配色跟随 **统计 Tab 页面主题**。

## 灵动岛与 Live Activity

支持的 iPhone（iOS 16.2+）可用。**番茄钟与学习计时各自拥有独立的 Live Activity**，请在各 Tab 齿轮设置中分别开启。

## 励志与心态

**1000+ 条精选语录**（JSON 语料，全在设备本地——简体 + 英文；「全部」筛选合计 **1000+**），六个触点。**两套语料池：**

| 语料池 | 用途 |
|--------|------|
| **弹窗 / 每日** | 启动弹窗、设置浏览、Live Activity hint、励志小组件 |
| **通知专用** | 仅后台计时结束推送——**中/英各 365 条通用短句**，与弹窗/每日语料分开 |

触点：

1. **启动弹窗** — 番茄 Tab 每次启动一次（全屏，按钮文案随语录语言）
2. **放弃计时** — 中途放弃会话时的全屏励志
3. **设置浏览** — **设置 → 励志语设置 → 励志语录大全**：全屏单条展示，底部换一条；可筛选语录语言（全部 / 仅简体 / 仅繁体 / 仅英文）。首次默认：英文 App → 仅英文，其余 → 全部
4. **Live Activity hint** — 锁屏计时时单行鼓励
5. **励志小组件** — 主屏每日一句；**iOS 17+** 可点 Widget 上 **刷新** 按钮即时换一句
6. **每日励志通知**（可选）——在固定时间重复推送一条 notification 语录

## 專注模式

### 番茄學習 — 經典專注循環

- **關聯定任務** — 可選 **關聯任務** 按鈕，記錄本次專注對應的倒數日；統計頁可按任務拆分。
- **時長完全可配** — 工作時分、短休息、長休息、長休息間隔（每 N 個番茄）。
- **點按編輯計時** — 點擊 H:M:S 大數字開啟滾輪選擇器；設定頁 Stepper 微調。
- **Chakra Petch 數字顯示** — 穩定無抖動的倒數排版，比例繼承自 Flutter 原版。
- **啟動勵志** — 進入番茄 Tab 時全螢幕展示一句語錄（每次啟動僅一次，切換 Tab 不重複打擾）。
- **放棄計時後** — 中途放棄工作階段時也可全螢幕展示勵志語錄（與啟動彈窗同款 UI）。
- **完成回饋** — 觸感、可選鬧鐘音、Live Activity **完成態**（10 秒後自動消失）。
- **階段完成確認**（可選）——開啟後每段工作/休息結束彈窗確認再進下一階段；關閉則鬧鐘後自動銜接。
- **防息屏** — 計時進行中可選保持螢幕常亮。
- **專注模式聯動**（可選）——開始計時時若系統專注模式未開啟，會彈窗提醒；可跳轉系統設定手動開啟（App 無法代開 Focus）。

### 計時學習 — 按你的節奏學

- 任意 **小時 + 分鐘** 時長。
- **關聯定任務** — 與番茄模式相同的 **關聯任務** 按鈕。
- 與番茄模式共享精緻 UI、滴答聲、背景通知與 Live Activity。
- 獨立主題色——預設海洋藍漸層，可完全自訂。
- **專注模式聯動**（可選）——與番茄模式相同，開始計時時可提醒開啟系統 Focus。

### 定任務 — 截止日期，一目了然

- **置頂區** — 置頂關鍵倒數日，始終顯示在列表頂部。
- **自訂順序** — 拖曳排序；自動切換為「自訂順序」模式。
- **排序 Menu** — 按日期、修改時間或手動順序（導覽列或任務設定）。
- **可選 iCloud 同步** — 定任務 → 齒輪 → **透過 iCloud 同步定任務**；統計 → 齒輪 → **透過 iCloud 同步學習統計**（**獨立開關**）。
- **關聯任務專注** — 計時畫面選擇關聯任務；定任務 **長按選單 → 番茄專注 / 計時專注** 跳轉對應 Tab 並綁定。
- **左滑編輯 / 置頂 / 刪除**；導覽列新增。編輯時可從 **9 種 accent 色** 中為任務選色（列表與定任務 Widget 同步顯示）。
- **到期提醒** — 僅當目標時間在未來時可開啟；過去時間會自動關閉提醒。
- **提醒管理**（定任務 → 齒輪 → 任務到期提醒）：按今天/明天/近 7 天/更晚分組；點進列可編輯或從 Sheet 底部刪除。

### 學習統計 — 看見進步

獨立 **統計** 儀表板（Swift Charts），擁有 **獨立頁面主題**（漸層背景 + 標題列）；工具列 **分享** 儀表板長圖 + **齒輪** 設定。

- **今日概覽**、**目標進度**、**週對比**、**月曆熱力圖**（上/下月、點月份 **選擇月份** Sheet 跳轉、**回到本月**；**未來日期**虛線邊框占位）、**成就**（9 枚，**新解鎖全螢幕慶祝**；可進詳情並分享列表/單枚）。
- 範圍/週期篩選（含 **自訂日期範圍**）；**星期與時段分布**（星期標籤隨 App 語言）、**按關聯任務拆分**、趨勢圖、最近列表（左滑刪除）。
- **週期報告** / **年度回顧** 專頁 + 圖片分享；**今日統計** 主畫面/鎖定畫面小工具。

**統計設定**（齒輪）：

| 分組 | 選項 |
|------|------|
| **主題樣式** | 標題列與背景漸層（統計 Tab 獨立 accent） |
| **目標** | 啟用目標、儀表板展示、日/週目標值 |
| **iCloud** | 可選同步工作階段 + 目標 + 成就狀態（`StudySession` / `StatsGoals` / `StatsAchievements`） |
| **成就** | 瀏覽已解鎖徽章 |
| **計數 / 顯示 / 資料** | 排除放棄、最短時長、記住篩選、**自訂日期**、CSV 匯入/匯出、保留策略、清空 |

**分享圖片**：應用推廣卡（設定）、**統計 Tab 儀表板長圖**（含目前篩選下的彙總、熱力圖、分布與趨勢）、週期報告、年度回顧、**成就列表/單枚**（含 App Store QR Code）；統計類分享圖配色跟隨 **統計 Tab 頁面主題**。
