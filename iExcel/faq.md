<section lang="en" markdown="1">

### Is iExcel fully offline?

Yes. All learning content — **265 functions**, **71 tips**, **44 lessons**, **23 quizzes**, and **114 shortcuts** — is bundled in the app. Browse, search, practice, and take quizzes without internet.

### Do I need an account?

No. **No registration, no login, no account.**

### Is iExcel affiliated with Microsoft Excel?

No. **Excel is a trademark of Microsoft.** iExcel is an independent learning tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Microsoft.

### What is the Formula Playground?

A **lightweight practice surface** on fixed sample cells. It demonstrates common patterns such as `SUM`, `AVERAGE`, `MAX`, `MIN`, `IF`, and simple cell addition. It is **not** a full Excel calculation engine and cannot replace a real spreadsheet.

### How does learning progress work?

Opening an item adds it to **Recents** and counts toward your **streak**, but does **not** increase the progress ring. Tap **Mark as mastered** when you understand it. Completing a **quiz** marks it mastered automatically. **Keyboard shortcuts** are excluded from the progress total so they do not dilute completion percentage.

### What does iCloud Sync cover?

When enabled, optional sync covers **favorites**, **recents**, **mastered items**, **quiz best scores**, **learning streak**, and **preferences** (language, appearance, tab visibility, widget theme). **Daily Function** is chosen per device and is **not** synced. Sync uses Apple **iCloud Key-Value Storage** under your Apple ID — default off.

### What widgets are available?

Four widgets: **Daily Function**, **Learning Progress**, **Favorites**, and **Quiz**. On iOS, Daily Function and Progress also support **Lock Screen** accessories. Open the app at least once so widgets can load a snapshot.

### How do I search on iOS 17?

iOS 18+ has a dedicated Search tab. On iOS 17, use **Home Screen Quick Actions** (long-press the app icon → Search), an **`iexcel://search?q=`** link, or the **Search Content** App Intent in Shortcuts.

### Can I clear my progress without losing favorites?

Yes. **Settings → Clear Progress** removes mastered items, quiz scores, recents, and streak — **favorites are kept**. **Clear Favorites** only removes the favorites list.

### How do I contact support?

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

### 爱表格是完全离线的吗？

是的。全部学习内容——**265 个函数**、**71 条技巧**、**44 门课程**、**23 套测验**与 **114 条快捷键**——均内置在应用中。浏览、搜索、练习与测验均无需联网。

### 需要注册账号吗？

不需要。**无需注册、登录或账号。**

### 爱表格与 Microsoft Excel 有关系吗？

没有。**Excel 是 Microsoft 的商标。** 爱表格是独立学习工具，与 Microsoft 无关联、无背书、无赞助关系。

### 公式演练场是什么？

基于固定示例单元格的**轻量练习区**。可演示 `SUM`、`AVERAGE`、`MAX`、`MIN`、`IF` 以及简单单元格相加等常见模式。它**不是**完整 Excel 计算引擎，不能替代真正的电子表格软件。

### 学习进度怎么计算？

打开条目会记入**最近浏览**并计入**连续天数**，但**不会**增加进度环。理解后请点**标记掌握**。完成**测验**会自动标记掌握。**快捷键**不计入进度总数，避免稀释完成度。

### iCloud 同步什么？

开启后，可选同步**收藏**、**最近浏览**、**已掌握条目**、**测验最高分**、**连续学习天数**与**偏好**（语言、外观、Tab 显示、小组件主题）。**每日一函数**按设备独立选择，**不同步**。同步使用 Apple **iCloud 键值存储**，默认关闭。

### 有哪些小组件？

四款：**每日一函数**、**学习进度**、**我的收藏**、**练习测验**。iOS 上每日一函数与学习进度还支持**锁屏配件**。请至少打开一次 App，以便小组件加载快照数据。

### iOS 17 上如何搜索？

iOS 18+ 有独立搜索 Tab。在 iOS 17 上，可使用**主屏快捷操作**（长按图标 → 搜索）、**`iexcel://search?q=`** 深链，或在快捷指令中调用 **Search Content** Intent。

### 可以清空进度但保留收藏吗？

可以。**设置 → 清空进度**会清除已掌握、测验分、最近浏览与连续天数——**收藏保留**。**清空收藏**仅清除收藏列表。

### 如何联系支持？

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
