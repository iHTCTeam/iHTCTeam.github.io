<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iExcel (also known as "爱表格" / "愛表格"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Summary

**iExcel does not require an account and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** All learning content is bundled in the app and runs fully offline. Optional **iCloud Sync** stores favorites, progress, and preferences in **your** iCloud Key-Value Storage under your Apple ID. **No ads. No tracking.**

## Data Collection

We do not operate analytics, advertising or tracking. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected by iHTCTeam (no name, email, phone number or iHTCTeam account).
- **No usage analytics** or crash-reporting SDKs are integrated.
- **Your learning data** stays on your devices and, if you enable sync, in **Apple iCloud** — not on iHTCTeam servers.
- **No cookies or third-party trackers** in the app.

## How the App Works

iExcel stores user-generated data locally on your device:

- All learning content (functions, tips, lessons, quizzes, shortcuts) is **bundled inside the app** and never fetched from an external content server.
- No internet connection is required to browse, search, practice, or take quizzes.
- Optional **iCloud sync** uses Apple's **NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore** when you are signed into iCloud and leave sync enabled. You can turn it off per device in Settings.
- Opening external links (e.g. feedback email, App Store pages) uses standard system networking; pages load directly from their hosts, not through any iHTCTeam server.

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Learning content library | Bundled functions, tips, lessons, quizzes, shortcuts |
| Favorites | Items you star |
| Recents | Recently viewed items (up to 30) |
| Mastered items | Items you mark as learned; quiz completion auto-marks |
| Quiz best scores | Highest score per quiz |
| Learning streak & last learn date | Consecutive-day tracking |
| Daily Function id & date | Featured function for the current calendar day |
| Preferences | Language, appearance, tab visibility, widget theme |
| Widget snapshot | Shared via App Group for Home / Lock Screen widgets |
| Onboarding completion | Whether the welcome guide has been finished |

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

When **iCloud Sync** is enabled and you are signed into iCloud:

- **Synced:** favorites, recents, mastered items, quiz scores, learning streak, last learn date, and preferences (language, appearance, category/favorites tab visibility, widget follows theme, sync flag).
- **Not synced:** Daily Function selection (chosen independently on each device).
- **Storage:** Apple **iCloud Key-Value Storage**, associated with your Apple ID.
- **Opt-out:** disable in **Settings → iCloud Sync** on any device; that device stops uploading and merging but keeps local data.
- iHTCTeam cannot read your iCloud container.

## App Group (Widget Extension)

iExcel uses an App Group (`group.com.iHTCboy.iExcel`) to share a small snapshot with its widget extension:

- Localized titles and summaries for Daily Function and Favorites
- Progress counts and streak for the Progress widget
- Appearance override for widget chrome

This data is written to a shared container **on your device only** and is never transmitted by iHTCTeam.

## Permissions

iExcel does **not** require camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library access for core learning features.

## In-App Purchase

iExcel includes an optional **tip / support the developer** purchase. All payment transactions are handled exclusively by **Apple's StoreKit framework**. iHTCTeam does not receive, process or store any payment card information or billing details.

## What iExcel Is Not

iExcel is a **learning reference and practice tool**. It does not open, edit, or sync your Excel workbooks. The Formula Playground evaluates a limited set of patterns on fixed sample data and is not a spreadsheet engine. Excel is a trademark of Microsoft; this app is not affiliated with Microsoft.

## Children's Privacy

iExcel does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we do not collect personal data, no special provisions are needed.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

**Last updated:** July 4, 2026

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
