<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iStore (also known as "爱商店" / "愛商店"). This page describes our policies regarding data collection, use and disclosure when you use our app.

## Summary

**iStore does not require an account and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** Region switching uses Apple's **official App Store redirect link** — iStore never sees or stores your Apple ID, password, or payment information. Your favorites, pinned regions, quick regions, switch history, and preferences are **saved on your device**; optional **iCloud Sync** can keep them in step across your devices through **iCloud**.

## Data Collection

We do not operate a backend that collects your personal information. Specifically:

- **No account** — No registration, email, or phone number is required.
- **No analytics or ads** — No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are integrated.
- **No credentials** — iStore never handles your Apple ID or password; switching happens on Apple's own screens.

## How the App Works

- **Switching regions** opens Apple's official App Store redirect link; the App Store handles the confirmation.
- **App Availability** checks use Apple's **public iTunes Lookup API** to see where an app is available. Only the app link/ID you enter is sent to Apple's public endpoint; no personal identifier is attached.
- Region flags, names, and map coordinates are **built into the app** and do not require a network request.

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Where it is kept | Purpose |
|------|------------------|---------|
| Favorites | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Regions you star |
| Pinned regions | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Regions kept at the top (subset of favorites) |
| Quick regions | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Regions for the Store "Popular" group, widgets, and quick actions |
| Switch history | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Recent switches (up to 30) |
| Last switched region | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Powers "Switch Last" control and widget |
| Cached storefront region | On your device | Your real App Store region, to highlight "current" |
| Appearance & language | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Light / Dark / System; language; region name display mode |
| Store layout & grid columns | On your device; optional **iCloud** | List / Grid / Cards preferences |
| Favorites display / group / sort | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Favorites tab preferences |
| Confirm-before-switch | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Whether to confirm before switching |
| Widget preferences | On your device; optional **iCloud** | Theme follow, custom widget regions |
| Onboarding & tips state | On your device | Whether you've seen the welcome guide / tips |
| Home Screen shortcuts | On your device | Quick-action region slots |
| Collapsed sections | On your device | Which Store sections are collapsed |
| iCloud sync toggle | On your device | Local preference for enabling sync |

## iCloud Sync

Optional iCloud sync keeps your favorites, pinned regions, quick regions, switch history, last switched region, and appearance / layout / favorites preferences in step across iPhone and iPad signed in to the same Apple ID. Data syncs through **your private iCloud account** (key-value store) — not iHTCTeam servers. Sync can be turned off in Settings.

## Permissions

iStore requests permissions only when needed:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Network | App Availability checks (Apple's public lookup API) and opening Apple's App Store redirect links |

iStore does not request access to your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera.

## Children's Privacy

iStore does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since iHTCTeam does not operate a data-collection backend, no special provisions are required beyond Apple's platform policies.

## Disclaimer

iStore is **not affiliated with Apple Inc.** Region switching uses Apple's official App Store redirect link. Availability and store rules are ultimately governed by Apple; refer to the official App Store for purchases and downloads.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
