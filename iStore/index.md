<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iStore (爱商店) is the fastest way to **switch your App Store region**. **No sign-in required** — switch your storefront in **one tap** across **175 regions** using Apple's **official** redirect link, browse and search every region by continent, **favorite and pin** the regions you use most, **check whether an app is available** in each region, pick a region on a **full-screen map**, and jump straight back with **Home Screen & Lock Screen widgets**, a **Control Center** control, **Spotlight**, **Shortcuts & Siri**, and a **Share Extension**. Everything stays private on your device, with **optional iCloud sync** across iPhone and iPad.

> **Note:** iStore is not affiliated with Apple Inc. Switching uses Apple's **official** App Store redirect link — iStore never sees or stores your Apple ID or password.

## Getting Started

1. Complete the short **welcome guide** on first launch (re-open anytime in **Settings → Guides**).
2. On the **Store** tab, browse regions by **Popular**, **Recent**, and **continent**, or use **Search** to find a region by name (English / 简体 / 繁体), code, or common keyword.
3. Tap a region and confirm to **switch your App Store storefront** — iStore opens Apple's official link and hands off to the App Store.
4. **Long-press** any region for quick actions: **Switch**, **Favorite**, **Pin / Unpin**, or **Copy link**.
5. Star regions to add them to **Favorites**; **pin** the ones you switch to most so they stay on top.
6. Open **App Availability** to paste an App Store link or ID and see **which regions the app is on** — then switch to any available region with one tap.
7. Try the **Map** tab (or Map Picker) to tap regions on a world map (Popular / Favorites / All).
8. Add **widgets** (Home & Lock Screen), the **Switch Last** Control Center control (iOS 18+), or ask **Siri** — and switch without opening the app first.

> Auto rating prompts are currently off; you can still rate from **Settings** or via the review deep link.

## Features

### One-Tap Region Switching

- **175 regions** across 7 continents, each with its flag and localized name
- Switches via Apple's **official `resetAndRedirect` link** — safe, no credentials required
- **Confirm before switch** (optional, on by default) to avoid mistaps
- **Switch history** — your last switches (up to 30), with one-tap re-switch; iCloud synced
- **Current storefront** highlight — iStore reads your real App Store region and marks it
- **Return guide** — a friendly banner helps you get back into iStore after switching

### Browse & Search

- **Store tab** grouped by **Popular**, **Recent**, and **continent**, with collapsible sections
- Three layouts — **List**, **Grid** (adjustable columns), and horizontal **Cards**
- Fast **search** by English / Simplified / Traditional name, Alpha-3 code, or keyword
- iPad **split-view** sidebar; iOS 18+ dedicated **Search tab** on iPhone

### Favorites & Pinning

- **Favorite** any region; a dedicated **Favorites tab** with **List / Grid / Slide** styles
- **Pin** important regions so they appear first everywhere, including widgets
- **Group, sort, drag-to-reorder**, and **batch select** (switch / pin / add to quick / remove)
- **Pinned order** manager to arrange your top regions exactly how you want

### App Availability

- Paste an **App Store link or numeric ID** to check **where an app is available**
- Scope the check to **Popular**, **Favorites**, or **All** regions
- Results are actionable — **switch** to any available region straight from the list
- Works from the app **and** the **Share Extension** (share any App Store link into iStore)

### Map Picker

- Pick regions on a **full-screen MapKit map** with pins for each region (also available from the main tab experience)
- Scope to **Popular**, **Favorites**, or **All**
- Tap a pin to switch — a great way to explore stores geographically

> Apple Watch companion is **not** included in the current build (source retained for a future richer watch experience).

### Widgets, Control Center & Spotlight

- **Home & Lock Screen widgets** — **Quick Regions**, **Recent**, and a **Custom** widget (iOS 17+ configurable)
- **Interactive widgets** (iOS 17+) — tap a region tile to switch
- **Control Center** — **Switch Last** control (iOS 18+)
- **Spotlight** — search all 175 regions from the Home Screen and switch directly

### Shortcuts, Siri & Quick Actions

- **`istore://` links** and **Siri / Shortcuts** — switch a region, switch your last region, switch a favorite, open the store, or check availability. See [Shortcut Links](url-scheme).
- **Home Screen Quick Actions** — long-press the icon for your top quick regions

### iCloud Sync & Privacy

- Optional **iCloud sync** keeps favorites, pinned regions, quick regions, switch history, and preferences in step across your devices
- **No account, no tracking, no ads** — data stays on your device or your private iCloud

### Personalization

- **Light / Dark / System** appearance; widgets can follow your theme
- **Region name display modes** and app language — **English · 简体中文 · 繁體中文**
- Configurable store layout, grid columns, favorites display, and confirmation behavior
