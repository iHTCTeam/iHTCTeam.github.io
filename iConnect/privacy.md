# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

[Back to iConnect Support](./)

---

iHTCTeam ("we", "us", or "our") built iConnect (also known as "爱联") as a paid app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

---

## Summary

**iConnect does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The app operates entirely on your local network and requires no account, no internet connection, and no cloud services.

---

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, phone number, or account).
- **No usage data** or analytics are collected.
- **No files or content** you transfer are accessible to us.
- **No cookies or tracking** technologies are used.
- **No third-party services** (advertising, analytics, crash reporting) are integrated.

---

## How the App Works

iConnect uses Apple MultipeerConnectivity framework to discover and communicate with nearby devices over your local Wi-Fi network. All file transfers are peer-to-peer and direct:

- Data travels only between your devices on the same local network.
- No data is routed through any external server.
- No internet connection is required for the app to function.

---

## Permissions

iConnect requests the following device permissions, only when needed:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Local Network Access | Discover and connect to nearby devices via Bonjour |
| Photo Library (Read) | Select photos and videos to send |
| Photo Library (Write) | Save received photos and videos |

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes. No data accessed through these permissions is transmitted externally.

---

## Data Storage

- **Transfer history** is stored locally on your device as a JSON file and is automatically deleted after 30 days. You can also clear it manually at any time in Settings.
- **App settings** (device name, preferences) are stored locally using UserDefaults on your device.
- No data is stored on any external server or cloud.

---

## Children's Privacy

iConnect does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, no special provisions are needed.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
