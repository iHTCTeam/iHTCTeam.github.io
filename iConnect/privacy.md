<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iConnect (also known as "爱联"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Summary

**iConnect does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.** Chat messages, push-to-talk audio and file transfers operate entirely on your local network. The app requires no account, no internet connection and no cloud services.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, phone number or account).
- **No usage data** or analytics are collected.
- **No chat messages, voice, files or other content** you send are accessible to us.
- **No cookies or tracking** technologies are used.
- **No third-party services** (advertising, analytics, crash reporting) are integrated.

## How the App Works

iConnect uses Apple MultipeerConnectivity framework to discover and communicate with nearby devices over your local Wi-Fi network. Chat, push-to-talk and file transfers are peer-to-peer and direct:

- Data travels only between your devices on the same local network.
- No data is routed through any external server.
- No internet connection is required for the app to function.

## Permissions

iConnect requests the following device permissions, only when needed:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Local Network Access | Discover and connect to nearby devices via Bonjour |
| Microphone | Push-to-talk (PTT) voice over the local P2P link |
| Photo Library (Read) | Select photos and videos to send |
| Photo Library (Write) | Save received photos and videos |

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes. No data accessed through these permissions is transmitted externally.

## Data Storage

- **Chat history** and **transfer history** are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded anywhere. Transfer logs are automatically deleted after 30 days; you can also clear history manually in Settings.
- **App settings** (device name, preferences) are stored locally using UserDefaults on your device.
- No data is stored on any external server or cloud.

## Children's Privacy

iConnect does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, no special provisions are needed.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

</section>

<section lang="zh-CN" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam（"我们"）为 iConnect（亦称"爱联"）这款应用开发并维护本页面，以告知你在使用应用过程中，我们关于个人数据收集、使用与披露的政策。

## 概要

**爱联不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。** 聊天消息、对讲语音与文件传输完全在本地网络运行。无需账号、无需联网、无需任何云服务。

## 数据收集

我们不收集任何数据。具体而言：

- **不收集任何个人信息**（无姓名、邮箱、电话或账号）。
- **不收集使用数据**或统计分析。
- **你发送的聊天、语音、文件或任何内容**我们均无法访问。
- **不使用 Cookie 或追踪**技术。
- **不集成任何第三方服务**（广告、统计、崩溃上报）。

## 应用工作原理

爱联使用 Apple MultipeerConnectivity 框架在本地 Wi-Fi 网络上发现和与附近设备通讯。聊天、对讲与文件传输均点对点直接进行：

- 数据只在同一本地网络的设备之间流动。
- 不经过任何外部服务器。
- 应用运行无需互联网连接。

## 权限

爱联仅在需要时请求以下设备权限：

| 权限 | 用途 |
|------------|---------|
| 本地网络访问 | 通过 Bonjour 发现并连接附近设备 |
| 麦克风 | 对讲（PTT）在本地 P2P 链路上传语音 |
| 相册（读取） | 选择要发送的照片与视频 |
| 相册（写入） | 保存接收到的照片与视频 |

这些权限仅用于上述目的。通过这些权限访问的数据不会向外传输。

## 数据存储

- **聊天记录**与**传输历史**存储在设备本地，不会上传。传输日志 30 天后自动删除；也可随时在设置中手动清除历史。
- **应用设置**（设备名、偏好）使用 UserDefaults 存储在设备本地。
- 不在任何外部服务器或云端存储数据。

## 儿童隐私

爱联不会有意收集任何人（包括 13 岁以下儿童）的个人数据。因为我们完全不收集数据，所以无需特别条款。

## 政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更都会更新本页并标注日期。建议你定期回访。

## 联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系：

- **邮箱：** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
