# iConnect / 爱联

**Local P2P File Sharing for iPhone, iPad & Mac**

iConnect is a peer-to-peer file sharing app that lets you share photos, videos, documents, and any file type directly between your Apple devices over local Wi-Fi. No cloud, no account, no internet required.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open iConnect on two devices connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
2. Devices will appear automatically on the radar screen.
3. Tap a device to connect.
4. Choose files, photos, or clipboard text to send.
5. The receiving device confirms and the transfer begins.
6. Completed files appear in the History tab.

---

## Features

- **Instant Discovery** - Nearby devices appear automatically on an animated radar screen.
- **Any File Type** - Photos, videos, PDFs, documents, audio, archives (ZIP, RAR, 7z), and more. Up to 2GB per file.
- **Batch Transfer** - Select and send up to 20 files at once.
- **Clipboard Sharing** - Send copied text directly to another device.
- **Real-Time Progress** - Live transfer speed and estimated time remaining.
- **Trust Devices** - Mark devices as trusted for automatic acceptance.
- **Transfer History** - 30-day log with Quick Look preview, save, and share.
- **Cross-Device** - Works between iPhone, iPad, and Mac in any combination.
- **Privacy First** - All transfers stay on your local network. No data leaves your devices.
- **Dark Mode** - Supports light and dark appearance.
- **Bilingual** - English and Simplified Chinese.
