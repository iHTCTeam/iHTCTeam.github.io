iYuanQu (爱元曲) is a **vertical Yuan-verse (元曲) studio** — focused on **散曲** (sanqu) and **杂剧** (zaju) verse, not a generic poetry list. **No account required.** All **8,969 qu works** are bundled on your device: **zero network requests** after install.

**At a glance:** 8,969 works · 45 curated-badge pieces (35 classic + 10 promoted) · 1,862 translated works · 227 authors · 12 gongdiao · 23 qupai · 162 zaju plays · daily qu with local reminders · continuous reading · 12 share-card styles · widgets · Siri · optional iCloud · **no ads, no tracking**.

## Getting Started

1. Open iYuanQu and complete (or skip) onboarding.
2. Start from **Daily Qu**, or browse by theme / author / play / gongdiao / qupai / full library.
3. On a detail page: immersive reading, TTS with **continuous reading**, share cards, favorites and playlists.
4. Optional: enable **daily reminder**, add **widgets**, or use **Siri / Shortcuts** (`iyuanqu://`).
