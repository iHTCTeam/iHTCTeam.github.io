<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam built iNutrition (爱营养 / 愛營養). This page describes our data practices.

## Summary

**iNutrition does not require an account, has no backend server, and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** Content is bundled offline. The AI Label Reader (iOS 26+) runs **on-device** — photos are not uploaded. Optional **iCloud Sync** for favorites and learning records uses **your private iCloud**. **No ads. No tracking.**

## Data Collection

- **No account** — no registration required.
- **No analytics or ads** — no third-party tracking SDKs.
- **No server** — we do not operate a backend that receives your data.

## AI Label Reader (iOS 26+)

Nutrition label photos are processed **entirely on your device** using Apple Intelligence. Images are **not uploaded** to iHTCTeam or third-party servers.

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Favorites | Foods, nutrients, articles you star |
| Learning records | Articles marked as learned |
| Browse history | Recent lookups |
| Preferences | Theme, accent color, layout, language |
| HealthKit permissions | Your choice in iOS Settings |

## iCloud Sync

Optional sync for favorites and learning records through your private iCloud key-value store. Can be disabled in Settings.

## Permissions

| Permission | When | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Camera | AI Label Reader | Photograph nutrition labels — on-device only |
| Photos | AI Label Reader | Pick existing label images — on-device only |
| HealthKit | Optional | Read/write dietary nutrition reference data |
| iCloud | Optional sync | Sync favorites and learning records |

## Contact

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

**爱营养无需账号、无后端服务器，不在 iHTCTeam 服务器上收集个人数据。** 内容内置离线。AI 营养表解读（iOS 26+）**在设备端运行**——照片不上传。可选 **iCloud 同步**收藏与学习记录，使用**你的私有 iCloud**。**无广告、无追踪。**
