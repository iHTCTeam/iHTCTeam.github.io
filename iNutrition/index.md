<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iNutrition (爱营养) helps you **understand nutrition science** — not count every calorie. **No account required.** Built around a **knowledge base**, **food lookup**, and **practical tools**, it answers the question: *what's actually in my food, and why does it matter?* Browse **19 original nutrition articles**, look up **2,160+ foods** with macro/micro charts, explore a **27-nutrient encyclopedia**, calculate **BMI / BMR / TDEE**, and on supported devices, photograph a nutrition label for **on-device AI interpretation** (iOS 26). Content is **bundled offline**; AI runs **on your device** with no uploads. **Widgets**, **HealthKit**, **Siri**, and **Spotlight** keep learning within reach. **No ads. No tracking.**

> **Note:** iNutrition is a reference and learning tool — **not** medical advice, a dietitian substitute, or a weight-loss打卡 app. *Understand nutrition, eat better.*

## Getting Started

1. Complete the **welcome guide** on first launch (re-open in **Settings → Guides**).
2. Read articles in **Knowledge** — mark topics as learned to track progress.
3. Search or browse **Foods** — tap any item for energy, macros, vitamins, minerals, %DV, and supply-ratio charts.
4. Open the **Nutrient Encyclopedia** for functions, sources, deficiency/excess signs, RDA, and rich-food reverse lookup.
5. Try **Tools** — BMI, BMR, TDEE, and personalized daily nutrient needs (DRI).
6. On **iOS 26+**, use **AI Label Reader** to photograph a nutrition facts panel — interpretation stays on-device.
7. Star foods, nutrients, and articles to **Favorites**; revisit **History**.
8. Add **widgets** for daily tips, random facts, or nutrient quick-look; optionally connect **HealthKit**.

## Features

### Knowledge Base

- **19 original structured articles** across 5 nutrition categories
- From macronutrients to vitamins & minerals — written for learners, not clinicians
- Mark articles as **learned**; track your reading progress
- **24 fun facts** for bite-sized discovery

### Food Lookup

- **2,160+ foods** with energy, protein, fat, carbs, fiber, vitamins, minerals
- **% Daily Value** and intuitive **supply-ratio ring charts**
- **8 food categories**; list layouts you can customize
- Data sourced from **USDA FoodData Central** (public domain) and China food composition references

### Nutrient Encyclopedia

- **27 nutrients** — function, food sources, deficiency & excess, RDA
- **Rich-food reverse lookup** — which foods contain the most of each nutrient
- Semantic color coding for energy, protein, fat, carbs, vitamins, minerals

### Practical Tools

- **BMI** · **BMR** · **TDEE** calculators
- **Personalized daily nutrient needs** based on DRI guidelines
- All tools work offline with bundled reference data

### AI Label Reader (iOS 26+)

- Photograph a **nutrition facts label** or ingredient list
- **On-device Apple Intelligence** — OCR + interpretation, no cloud upload
- Understand additives, serving sizes, and key nutrients at a glance

### Widgets & System Integration

- **Widgets** — daily tip, random fact, nutrient quick look, favorites shortcut (home, lock & control)
- **Siri & Shortcuts** — open label reader, search foods, nutrition knowledge
- **Spotlight** — index foods, nutrients, and articles for system search
- **`inutrition://` deep links** — see [Shortcut Links](url-scheme)
- Optional **HealthKit** read/write for dietary nutrition reference data

### Personalization

- **5 accent color themes** — Leaf, Berry, Citrus, Ocean, Grape
- Light / Dark / System appearance
- Trilingual UI: English · 简体中文 · 繁體中文
- Optional **iCloud sync** for favorites and learning records

### Privacy

- **No account · no backend server · no ads · no tracking**
- Content bundled offline; AI runs on-device (iOS 26)
- Optional iCloud for your own favorites and progress only
