<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iTer (also known as "爱技术"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Summary

**iTer does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.** The app operates entirely on your local device and requires no account, no internet connection and no cloud services.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, phone number or account).
- **No usage data** or analytics are collected.
- **No questions, notes or any content** you browse in the app is tracked or accessible to us.
- **No cookies or tracking** technologies are used.
- **No third-party services** (advertising, analytics, crash reporting) are integrated.
- **No third-party SDKs** are embedded in the app.

## How the App Works

iTer stores all data locally on your device:

- All interview questions, concept guides and app content are bundled within the app itself.
- Data never leaves your device.
- No internet connection is required for any feature of the app.
- No server communication occurs at any time.

## Permissions

iTer does not request any special device permissions. The app does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library or any other sensitive system resource.

## Data Storage

- **App content** (interview questions, concept guides) is bundled as read-only resources within the app.
- **App preferences** (theme, language, list layout, quiz scope, shortcuts) are stored locally using UserDefaults on your device.
- **Learning progress** (favorites, recent views, quiz scores, wrong-book records, mastery marks) is stored locally on your device.
- **Widget data** (question catalog mirror, theme snapshot, language code, quiz scope) is written to a shared **App Group** container on your device so the Widget extension can read it offline. This data never leaves your device.
- No data is stored on any external server or cloud service.

## Children's Privacy

iTer does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, no special provisions are needed.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

</section>
