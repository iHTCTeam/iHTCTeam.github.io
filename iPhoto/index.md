<section lang="en" markdown="1">

**iPhoto (爱修图)** is a privacy-first **local photo studio** for iPhone and iPad. Capture with live face beauty, edit non-destructively, strip metadata before you share — **no account, no photo upload, no ads, no tracking.**

**At a glance:** live beauty camera · non-destructive editor · curated filters · per-face retouch · privacy export · Home & Lock Screen widgets · Siri, Shortcuts & `iphoto://` deep links · optional iCloud preferences sync.

> iPhoto is an independent app and is **not affiliated with Apple Inc.**

---

## Part 1 — First Launch

1. Download from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6787360978) and open **iPhoto**.
2. Complete the short **welcome guide** (you can replay it later in **Settings → Feature Guide**).
3. When prompted, allow **Camera** (for Capture) and **Photos** (for import / optional save).
4. Start from the **Capture** tab (default when Capture is enabled), or switch to **Library**.

No sign-in screen — you are ready to create immediately.

---

## Part 2 — Library

### Browse & organize

- Import from the system **Photos** app, or shoot in Capture.
- Switch **grid / list** layout; adjust column count in Settings.
- Filter by **All · Edited · Captured · Imported · Favorites**.
- **Search** projects; tap any item to open the editor.
- Favorites and recent work stay on device for quick return.

### Tips

- Tap **+** / import to bring photos in without leaving iPhoto.
- Edits are stored as parameters — the **original file is kept** for imported projects.

---

## Part 3 — Capture with Live Beauty

1. Open the **Capture** tab.
2. Toggle **Beauty** on. Fine-tune **Amount** and tools: Smooth, Whiten, Slim Face, Eyes, Teeth, Blush, Lipstick (with color picks).
3. Optional: flash, countdown, grid, front mirror, and live **filters**.
4. The live preview keeps the **real camera background** and only overlays the face region when a face is detected.
5. Tap the shutter. If beauty or a filter is on, the look is **baked once** into the saved original so the editor will not double-apply it.
6. In Settings you can **Remember beauty recipe** across sessions (default on).

> Simulator has no camera — test Capture on a real device.

---

## Part 4 — Non-Destructive Editor

Open any project. Tools along the bottom:

| Tool | What you can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Adjust** | Exposure, contrast, warmth and more |
| **Filters** | Dozens of curated looks — film, portrait, landscape, food, night, vintage, vivid, B&W |
| **Beauty** | Presets + per-face controls when multiple faces are found |
| **Crop** | Crop, rotate, flip |
| **Effects** | Background blur and more |
| **Frame / Sticker / Text** | Finish your look |
| **Privacy** | Strip EXIF, GPS and device metadata |

- **Undo / Redo** anytime; **hold** the preview to compare with the original.
- Beauty uses **on-device Vision** — landmarks never leave your phone.
- Autoselect **All faces** or a single face to dial parameters independently.

---

## Part 5 — Export & Privacy

1. Tap export and pick **Original / High / Medium / Low** quality.
2. Optionally **Save to Photos** (Settings toggle).
3. Enable **Confirm before export** if you want a safety check.
4. Turn on **Strip Metadata** before sharing travel photos or portraits.

Photos stay in the app sandbox (and optional Photos album) — **never uploaded** by iPhoto for analysis.

Share or bookmark `iphoto://` links (Capture, Library, Guides, Editor by id, and more). Full list: [Shortcut Links](url-scheme).

- **Appearance** — Light / Dark / System; widgets can follow theme
- **Language** — English · 简体中文 · 繁體中文 (in-app preference)
- **Capture** — grid, front mirror, remember beauty, show/hide Capture tab

- **iCloud Sync** — preferences only (never photo files)

**爱修图（iPhoto）** 是面向 iPhone 与 iPad 的**隐私优先本地照片工作室**。拍摄即可实时美颜，非破坏性修图，分享前可清除元数据——**无需账号、不上传原图、无广告、无追踪。**

**一眼看懂：** 实时美颜相机 · 非破坏编辑器 · 精选滤镜 · 按脸美颜 · 隐私导出 · 主屏与锁屏小组件 · Siri、快捷指令与 `iphoto://` 深链 · 可选 iCloud 偏好同步。

> 爱修图为独立第三方应用，**与 Apple Inc. 无关联。**

---

## 一、首次打开

1. 从 [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6787360978) 下载并打开 **爱修图**。
2. 完成简短**欢迎引导**（之后可在 **设置 → 功能引导** 重看）。
3. 按提示授予**相机**（拍摄）与**相册**（导入 / 可选保存）权限。
4. 默认进入**拍摄**页（若开启拍摄 Tab），或切换到**资源库**。

没有登录页——装好就能用。

---

## 二、资源库

### 浏览与整理

- 从系统**相册**导入，或在拍摄页直接拍。
- **网格 / 列表**布局可切换；列数可在设置中调整。
- 按 **全部 · 已编辑 · 拍摄 · 导入 · 收藏** 筛选。
- **搜索**项目，点开即可继续修图。
- 收藏与最近项目保存在本机，随时回来。

### 小建议

- 用导入把照片带进爱修图，不必离开 App。
- 导入项目的**原图会保留**；编辑以参数形式保存（非破坏）。

---

## 三、拍摄与实时美颜

1. 打开**拍摄**页。
2. 开启**美颜**，调节**总强度**与细项：磨皮、美白、瘦脸、大眼、美牙、腮红、唇彩（可调色）。
3. 可选：闪光灯、倒计时、网格、前置镜像、实时**滤镜**。
4. 实时预览保持**真实相机背景**，仅在检测到人脸时叠加人脸区域效果。
5. 按下快门。若开启了美颜或滤镜，效果会**一次烘焙**进入库原图，避免编辑器再次叠加。
6. 设置里可开启**记住美颜配方**（默认开）。

> 模拟器无相机——请在真机测试拍摄。

---

## 四、非破坏性编辑器

打开任一项目，底部工具：

| 工具 | 能做什么 |
|------|----------|
| **调节** | 曝光、对比度、色温等 |
| **滤镜** | 数十款精选：胶片、人像、风景、美食、夜景、复古、鲜艳、黑白 |
| **美颜** | 预设；多人脸时可按脸单独调 |
| **裁剪** | 裁剪、旋转、翻转 |
| **特效** | 背景虚化等 |
| **边框 / 贴纸 / 文字** | 完成成片氛围 |
| **隐私** | 去除 EXIF、定位与设备信息 |

- 随时**撤销 / 重做**；**按住**预览对比原图。
- 美颜使用**端侧 Vision**，关键点不离开设备。
- 可选**全部脸**或单张脸独立调节参数。

---

## 五、导出与隐私

1. 点导出，选择 **原图 / 高 / 中 / 低** 画质。
2. 可选**写入系统相册**（设置开关）。
3. 可开**导出前确认**，避免误触。
4. 分享旅行照或人像前，建议打开**清除元数据**。

照片只留在 App 沙盒（以及你选择的系统相册）——爱修图**不会上传**照片去做分析。
