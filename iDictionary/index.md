<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iDictionary (爱字词典) is a **fully offline Chinese character learning dictionary** — your pocket library of Chinese writing. **No account required.** After a one-time database setup on first launch, everything runs on your device: **20,000+ hanzi**, **320,000+ words**, **49,000+ idioms**, and **9,500+ stroke-order SVG animations**. Look up by pinyin, radical, stroke count, handwriting, or camera OCR; listen to **Mandarin TTS**; flip **daily character** and **daily idiom** cards; and stay close to learning with **widgets**, **Live Activity**, **Control Center**, **Siri**, **Shortcuts**, and **Spotlight**. Optional **iCloud sync** for favorites — default off. **No ads. No tracking.**

## Getting Started

1. On first launch, wait briefly while the offline database inflates (~155 MB) — then you're fully offline forever.
2. Complete the **welcome guide** (re-open anytime in **Settings → Guides**).
3. Use **Search** for characters, words, idioms, or pinyin — or browse by **radical**, **stroke count**, **frequency**, and **polyphones**.
4. Tap any character for detail: pinyin, radical, strokes, structure, variants, definitions, citations, and **stroke-order animation**.
5. Try **Handwriting** lookup or **OCR camera** scan when you see a character you don't know.
6. Open **Daily Character** or **Daily Idiom** flip cards on the home screen.
7. Star entries to **Favorites**; revisit **History** anytime.
8. Add **widgets** (home & lock screen), enable **Live Activity** for today's character, or ask **Siri** to look up a character.

## Features

### Offline Dictionary at Scale

- **20,000+ hanzi** · **320,000+ words** · **49,000+ idioms** — unified FTS5 search
- Character detail: pinyin, radical, stroke count, structure, variants, definitions, example citations
- **9,500+ stroke-order SVG animations** — watch every stroke, step by step
- Browse indexes: radical, strokes, frequency, polyphones, idiom pinyin
- Trilingual UI (English · 简体中文 · 繁體中文); content in Simplified with live 简→繁 conversion

### Multimodal Lookup

- **Handwriting input** — draw a character to search
- **OCR camera scan** — point at printed text to recognize characters
- **Mandarin TTS** with auto-read and adjustable speed
- **Daily Character** & **Daily Idiom** flip cards for bite-sized learning
- **Random Character** — shake the dice or use Control Center (iOS 18+)

### Learning & Personalization

- **Favorites** & **browse history**
- Optional **iCloud KV sync** for favorites and preferences (default off)
- Configurable TTS, theme, and display options

### Widgets, Live Activity & System Integration

- **Widgets** — daily character, daily idiom, learning overview + lock screen accessories
- **Live Activity** (iOS 16.2+) — today's character on Dynamic Island & lock screen
- **Control Center** (iOS 18+) — random character control
- **Siri & Shortcuts** — lookup, daily character, random, open search
- **Spotlight** (iOS 17+) — character index with deep links to detail
- **Home Screen Quick Actions** — up to 4 customizable shortcuts
- **`idict://` deep links** — see [Shortcut Links](url-scheme)

### Privacy

- **No account · no registration · no ads · no tracking**
- All dictionary data stays on your device after first-launch setup
- Optional iCloud sync for your own favorites only
