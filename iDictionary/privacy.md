<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam built iDictionary (爱字词典 / 愛字詞典). This page describes our data practices.

## Summary

**iDictionary does not require an account and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** All dictionary content is stored on your device after first-launch setup. Optional **iCloud Sync** can keep favorites and preferences in step across your devices through **your private iCloud** — default **off**. **No ads. No tracking.**

## Data Collection

- **No account** — no registration, email, or phone number required.
- **No analytics or ads** — no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- **No cloud dictionary** — lookups never leave your device (except optional iCloud for your own favorites).

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Inflated dictionary database | Offline hanzi, words, idioms, stroke SVG |
| Favorites | Characters, words, idioms you star |
| Browse history | Recent lookups |
| Preferences | Theme, language, TTS settings |
| Onboarding state | Whether you've seen guides |
| iCloud sync toggle | Local preference for enabling sync |

## iCloud Sync

Optional iCloud sync keeps favorites and preferences in step across devices signed in to the same Apple ID. Data syncs through **your private iCloud key-value store** — not iHTCTeam servers. **Disabled by default.**

## Permissions

| Permission | When | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Camera | OCR lookup | Recognize characters in photos — processed on-device |
| Microphone | Not used | — |
| iCloud | Optional sync | Sync your favorites across devices |

## Children's Privacy

iDictionary does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

iHTCTeam 开发了爱字词典。本页说明我们的数据处理方式。

## 摘要

**爱字词典无需账号，不在 iHTCTeam 服务器上收集个人数据。** 首次解压后，所有词典内容保存在你的设备上。可选 **iCloud 同步**可通过**你的私有 iCloud** 在多设备间同步收藏与偏好——**默认关闭**。**无广告、无追踪。**

## 数据收集

- **无账号**——无需注册、邮箱或手机号。
- **无分析或广告**——不集成第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。
- **无云端词典**——查词不离开设备（可选 iCloud 仅同步你的收藏）。

## 设备本地存储

| 数据 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| 解压后的词典数据库 | 离线汉字、词语、成语、笔顺 SVG |
| 收藏 | 你加星标的条目 |
| 浏览历史 | 最近查询 |
| 偏好设置 | 主题、语言、朗读设置 |
| 引导状态 | 是否已看过使用指南 |
| iCloud 同步开关 | 是否启用同步 |

## iCloud 同步

可选 iCloud 同步让登录同一 Apple ID 的设备间保持收藏与偏好一致。数据通过**你的私有 iCloud 键值存储**同步——不经过 iHTCTeam 服务器。**默认关闭。**

## 权限

| 权限 | 时机 | 原因 |
|------|------|------|
| 相机 | 拍照识字 | 在设备端识别汉字 |
| 麦克风 | 不使用 | — |
| iCloud | 可选同步 | 跨设备同步收藏 |

## 儿童隐私

爱字词典不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

## 变更

我们可能更新本政策。继续使用即视为接受变更。

## 联系

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
