<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iCoder (previously iLeetCoder) is an algorithm learning app with **999 LeetCode problems** designed for coding interview preparation. Browse by number, tag, or company; track learning progress; take a daily quiz; and use home-screen and lock-screen widgets — fully offline on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

*Note: Due to copyright restrictions, the app is not currently available in mainland China and is only offered in regions outside the Chinese mainland.*

## Getting Started

1. Open iCoder and browse the **Problems** tab, or switch to **Categories** for tag- and company-based training.
2. Tap any problem to read its description, adjust font size, mark **Favorite**, and set **Learning Status** (Learned / Mastered).
3. Use the **Daily Quiz** tab for 10 questions per day; review mistakes in the wrong-answer book under **Settings**.
4. Open **Settings → Learning Records** to browse every problem you have marked learned or mastered, with filters.
5. Optionally enable **iCloud Sync** in Settings to keep favorites, learning status, and the wrong-answer book in sync across your Apple devices.
6. Add **Widgets** to your Home Screen or Lock Screen for a daily problem, quiz streak, favorites, and more.
7. On Mac or iPad with a keyboard, use **⌘F** to search and **⌘1–4** to switch tabs; on Mac, **Esc** steps back through panels before navigating away.
8. Use **`iLeetCoder://`** deep links or Home Screen Quick Actions — see the [URL Scheme Reference](url-scheme).

## Highlights

- **macOS Native** — Same SwiftUI codebase for iOS 16+ and macOS 13+: menu-bar shortcuts, capsule search bar under tabs, draggable/resizable floating solution panel, native share sheet, and hierarchical **Esc** back navigation.
- **Traditional Chinese Content** — When the UI is set to Traditional Chinese, problem titles, descriptions, and tags are converted on the fly; search matches both Simplified and Traditional keywords.
- **Smarter Widgets** — Daily Problem widget supports difficulty/tag filters on iOS 17+; Company Spotlight shuffle; improved Difficulty Glance layout; lock-screen accessory widgets.
- **SwiftUI Experience** — Redesigned About and widget guides, shareable long images, font-size slider, clearable recent browsing, and category navigation fixes.

## Features

- **999 Algorithm Problems** — Curated LeetCode problems covering arrays, strings, trees, graphs, dynamic programming, and more. List or grid layout with difficulty, VIP, and favorite filters.
- **Categories & Companies** — Train by algorithm tag or IT company interview sets; list/grid layout matches the Problems tab.
- **Powerful Search** — Find problems by number, title, or keyword. Traditional Chinese queries match Simplified content automatically.
- **Learning Progress** — Mark problems as Learned or Mastered; track learned and mastered rates from the Problems tab and Settings stats. **Learning Records** lists every marked problem with learned/mastered filters.
- **iCloud Sync** — Optional toggle in Settings syncs favorites, learning status, and the wrong-answer book across devices signed in to the same iCloud account.
- **Daily Quiz** — 10 questions per day with streak tracking and a wrong-answer book linked to problem details.
- **Screenshot & Long-Image Share** — Capture a problem as an image for Messages, email, or study groups.
- **Solution Browser** — Open official solution pages in-app (Safari view on iOS; floating WebView on Mac with zoom and system-browser option).
- **15 Theme Presets** — Custom accent colors; Light, Dark, or System appearance; optional widget background that follows the app theme (iOS).
- **URL Scheme & Quick Actions** — `iLeetCoder://` deep links for problems, quiz, favourites, tags, and companies; configurable Home Screen shortcuts (iOS).
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** — ⌘F search, ⌘1–4 switch tabs; Mac Esc closes font bar → share → solution panel → navigation back.
- **Multilingual UI** — English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese, independent of system language.
- **Offline** — All problems and quiz content are bundled locally. Widgets work offline too.
- **No Ads** — The app never shows ad pop-ups.

## Widgets

iCoder offers **7 home-screen widgets** and **lock-screen accessories** to track your study progress at a glance. See **Settings → Widgets → How to Use** in the app for the full illustrated guide.

### How to Add

1. Long-press your Home Screen (or Lock Screen) to enter edit mode.
2. Tap **+** and search for "iCoder."
3. Choose a widget, pick a size, and tap **Add Widget**.
4. On iOS 17+, edit the **Daily Problem** widget to filter by difficulty and tags.

### Available Widgets

| Widget | Description | Size |
|--------|-------------|------|
| Daily Problem | Featured problem each day. iOS 17+: filter by difficulty/tags; tap **⇄** to shuffle. | Small, Medium |
| Company Spotlight | A featured company plus a sample interview question; tap **↻** to shuffle (iOS 17+). | Small, Medium |
| Difficulty Glance | Easy / Medium / Hard distribution with color bar (small) or row details (medium). | Small, Medium |
| Quiz Glance | Today's quiz score, completion status, and streak. | Small |
| Favorites Glance | Up to three favorites plus total count. | Small, Medium |
| Wrong Book Glance | Recent wrong answers and count. | Small, Medium |
| Learning Progress | Learned and mastered counts with percentages. | Small, Medium |
| Lock Screen | Compact daily problem, quiz, favorites, or wrong-book summaries. | Lock Screen |

Widgets deep-link into problem details, the quiz, favorites, wrong book, or company categories. Enable **Widget background follows theme** in Settings (iOS) for a gradient that matches the app.

爱算法（iCoder，曾用名 iLeetCoder）是一款算法学习应用，包含 **999 道 LeetCode 算法题**，专为编程面试准备而设计。按编号、标签或公司浏览；跟踪学习进度；每日测验；主屏与锁屏小组件——在 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac 上完全离线运行。

*注：中国内地因版权问题，暂不支持，仅非中国大陆地区提供。*

## 快速上手

1. 打开爱算法，在**题目** Tab 浏览列表，或切换到**分类** Tab 按标签、公司专项训练。
2. 点按任意题目查看描述，调节字号，标记**收藏**，并设置**学习状态**（已学习 / 已掌握）。
3. 在**每日测验** Tab 完成每天 10 道题；错题可在**设置**中的错题本回顾。
4. 在**设置 → 学习记录**查看所有已标记为已学习/已掌握的题目，支持筛选。
5. 可在**设置**中开启 **iCloud 同步**，在登录同一 iCloud 账户的设备间同步收藏、学习状态与错题本。
6. 添加**小组件**到主屏幕或锁屏，快速查看每日一题、测验连续天数、收藏等。
7. 在 Mac 或连接键盘的 iPad 上，**⌘F** 搜索、**⌘1–4** 切换 Tab；Mac 上 **Esc** 会依次关闭字号条、分享、题解浮窗后再返回上一级。
8. 使用 **`iLeetCoder://`** 深度链接或主屏快捷方式——见 [URL Scheme 文档](url-scheme)。

## 亮点

- **macOS 原生体验** — iOS 16+ 与 macOS 13+ 同仓 SwiftUI：菜单栏快捷键、Tab 下胶囊搜索条、可拖动缩放题解浮窗、原生分享、**Esc** 分级返回。
- **繁体中文内容** — 界面选繁体时，题目标题、正文、标签展示层简转繁；搜索同时匹配简繁关键词。
- **小组件增强** — 每日一题支持 iOS 17+ 难度/标签筛选；公司热门换题；难度速览布局优化；锁屏 accessory 小组件。
- **SwiftUI 体验** — 重做关于页与小组件说明、分享长图、字号滑块、最近浏览一键清空、分类导航优化。

## 功能特性

- **999 道算法题库** — 精选 LeetCode 题目，涵盖数组、字符串、树、图、动态规划等；列表/网格切换，难度、VIP、收藏过滤。
- **分类与公司** — 按算法标签或 IT 公司面试题专项训练；与题目 Tab 共用列表/网格布局偏好。
- **强大搜索** — 按编号、标题、关键词查找；繁体关键词可搜到简体题库内容。
- **学习进度** — 标记已学习 / 已掌握；在题目 Tab 与设置统计中查看已学习、已掌握比例。**学习记录** 列出全部已标记题目，可按已学习/已掌握筛选。
- **iCloud 同步** — 设置中可选开启，通过 iCloud 在多台 Apple 设备间同步收藏、学习状态与错题本。
- **每日测验** — 每天 10 题，连续天数统计，错题本可跳转题目详情。
- **截图与长图分享** — 截取题目分享给朋友或学习小组。
- **题解浏览** — 应用内打开官方题解（iOS 为 Safari 视图；Mac 为右下角浮动 WebView，可放大/缩小、系统浏览器打开）。
- **15 套主题色** — 自定义强调色；浅色 / 深色 / 跟随系统；小组件背景可跟随 App 主题（iOS）。
- **URL Scheme 与快捷操作** — `iLeetCoder://` 深度链接打开题目、测验、收藏、标签与公司筛选；可配置主屏快捷方式（iOS）。
- **键盘快捷键** — ⌘F 搜索、⌘1–4 切换 Tab；Mac 上 Esc 依次关闭字号条 → 分享 → 题解浮窗 → 导航返回。
- **多语言界面** — 英文、简体中文、繁体中文，独立于系统语言。
- **完全离线** — 题目与测验内容均打包在本地；小组件同样离线可用。
- **无广告** — 应用内永不显示广告弹窗。
