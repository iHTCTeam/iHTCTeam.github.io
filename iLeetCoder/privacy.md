<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iCoder (also known as "iLeetCoder" / "爱算法" / "愛算法"). This page describes our policies regarding data collection, use and disclosure when you use our app.

## Summary

**iCoder does not collect, store or transmit personal data to iHTCTeam's servers.** All algorithm problems and quiz content are bundled on your device. Core features work offline with no account required. Learning data stays on your device; optional **iCloud Sync** uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value storage under your Apple ID.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data from you. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is sent to iHTCTeam (no name, email, phone number or account on our servers).
- **No usage analytics** or crash-reporting SDKs from third parties.
- **No advertising** or tracking technologies.

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is created by iCoder and stored locally (UserDefaults and App Group shared storage):

| Data | Storage | User Control |
|------|---------|--------------|
| App preferences (theme, language, layout) | Local | Change in Settings; reset by deleting the app |
| Favorites & learning status | Local | Manage in app; reset status per problem |
| Recent browsing | Local | Clear in Browse tab or Settings |
| Daily quiz scores & wrong-answer book | Local | Clear wrong book in Settings |
| Widget preferences | App Group (shared with Widget Extension) | Change in Settings (iOS) |

No data is uploaded to iHTCTeam or shared with third parties.

## Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable **iCloud Sync** in Settings, favorites, learning status, the wrong-answer book, and daily quiz progress sync across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac devices signed in to the same Apple ID via **iCloud Key-Value storage** (keys such as `iCloud.com.iHTCboy.iLeetCoder.cloudSnapshot.v1`). Data stays in **your private iCloud account** through Apple's infrastructure — not iHTCTeam servers. You can turn sync off anytime; local data remains on each device, and you may optionally remove the copy stored in iCloud.

## Permissions

iCoder does not require camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library access for core features. Opening solution pages or external links uses the network when you choose to view online content.

## Children's Privacy

iCoder does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since iHTCTeam does not operate a data-collection backend, no special provisions are required beyond Apple's platform policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

</section>
