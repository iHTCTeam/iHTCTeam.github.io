<section lang="en" markdown="1">

Tang Poems 300 (爱唐诗三百首) is an **immersive Tang poetry learning studio** — not just another poem list. **No account required.** All **318 poems** from the classic *唐诗三百首* are bundled on your device: **zero network requests** after install. Read with pinyin, translation, annotations, and appreciation; listen with **karaoke-style word-by-word TTS** and lock-screen Now Playing; study with **fade, fill-blank, recitation test, quiz, and 飞花令**; discover a **contextual daily poem** tied to solar terms, festivals, and seasons. **6 widgets**, **Siri**, optional **iCloud progress**, and shareable Tang-style cards. **No ads. No tracking. Data Not Collected.**

> **Note:** Tang Poems 300 is an independent education app. Poem data is compiled from open-source references (jingmo-data, chinese-poetry) with in-app attribution.

## Getting Started

1. Complete the **6-page welcome guide** on first launch (re-open in **Settings → Guides**).
2. Browse by **genre**, **poet**, **theme**, or the classic **catalog** — or use **Search** across titles, lines, and poets.
3. Tap any poem for **immersive reading** — vertical or horizontal layout, pinyin, translation, annotations, background story, appreciation, and critical commentary (99%+ coverage).
4. Tap **Read Aloud** for karaoke-style highlighting; adjust speed, pitch, and voice; enjoy **continuous playlist** with background playback.
5. Open **Study** — try fade mode, fill-in-the-blank, recitation test, or quiz; play **飞花令** with character search.
6. Check **Daily Poem** — context-aware recommendations by solar term, festival, season, and time of day.
7. Star poems to **Favorites**; track **4-level mastery** and study streaks.
8. Add **6 widget types** (including StandBy & lock screen); ask **Siri** for today's poem or a random verse.
