iHTCTeam built iTangPoems (爱唐诗 / 愛唐詩).

## Summary

**Data Not Collected.** iTangPoems makes **zero outbound network requests** after install. **No account. No ads. No analytics SDK.** All poem data is stored locally. Optional **iCloud Sync** for favorites and study progress uses **your private iCloud** only.

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Poem database | 318 bundled poems |
| Favorites | Poems you star |
| Study progress | Mastery levels, streaks, quiz results |
| Preferences | Theme, TTS settings, reading layout |
| Notification settings | Daily poem reminders (local only) |

## iCloud Sync

Optional sync for favorites and study progress through your private iCloud key-value store.

## Permissions

| Permission | When | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Notifications | Daily poem reminder | Local scheduling only — no server |
| iCloud | Optional sync | Sync favorites and progress |

## Contact

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
