<section lang="en" markdown="1">

### Is the app fully offline?

Yes. All **318 poems** are bundled in the app. After install, the app makes **zero outbound network requests** — no account, no ads, no analytics.

### How is this different from other poetry apps?

iTangPoems is a **learning studio**, not a static list. It combines **karaoke-style read-aloud**, **immersive reading** with annotations, **study modes** (fade, fill-blank, quiz, 飞花令), and **contextual daily poems** — plus 6 widgets and Siri integration.

### How many poems are included?

The complete **唐诗三百首** — **318 poems** by **76 Tang poets**, with **958 famous lines** indexed.

### How does read-aloud work?

Built-in **TTS** highlights each character as it speaks — like karaoke. Adjust speed, pitch, and voice. Supports background playback and lock-screen Now Playing controls.

### What study modes are available?

**Fade** (lines disappear as you memorize), **fill-in-the-blank**, **recitation test**, **quiz**, and **飞花令** character game. Track **4-level mastery** and study streaks.

### What is the daily poem?

A context-aware recommendation based on **solar terms**, **festivals**, **seasons**, and **time of day** — with optional local notifications.

### What widgets are available?

**6 widget types** — daily poem, favorites, progress, poet spotlight, lock screen, and StandBy accessories. iOS 18+ Control Center control.

### What does iCloud Sync cover?

Optional sync for **favorites** and **study progress** (mastery levels, streaks). Uses your private iCloud.

### How do I contact support?

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
