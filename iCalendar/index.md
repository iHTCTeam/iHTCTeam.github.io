**Plan every day with lunar precision — on your iPhone or iPad, entirely offline.**

iCalendar (爱日历 / 愛日曆) is a comprehensive Chinese lunar calendar covering **1891–2100**. Solar terms, traditional festivals, stems-branches, full-screen almanac, tear-off calendar, countdown days, date-interval calculator (Gregorian or lunar), month share images, and **20 home-screen widgets** plus **5 lock-screen accessories** — all on-device with zero third-party dependencies.

## Getting Started

1. Open iCalendar and browse the monthly calendar view.
2. Tap any date to see the lunar date, solar term, festival, and almanac details.
3. Swipe left/right to switch months; tap the header to return to today.
4. Go to **Countdown** tab to add important events with reminders.
5. Add widgets from your home screen or lock screen for quick glances.

## Features

- **Public & Lunar Calendar** — Full conversion between Gregorian and Chinese lunar dates, covering 1891–2100.
- **24 Solar Terms** — Accurate solar term calculation with traditional descriptions.
- **Traditional Festivals** — Chinese New Year, Mid-Autumn, Dragon Boat, Qingming, and more.
- **Stems-Branches & Zodiac** — Year, month, and day heavenly-stems and earthly-branches, plus zodiac information.
- **Almanac (黄历)** — Daily auspicious and inauspicious activities guide.
- **Countdown Days** — Events with categories, pin, list or grid layout, 9 display modes (days through seconds), solar/lunar dates, reminders, share cards, optional iCloud sync, and **JSON import/export** for backup.
- **Show on Calendar** — Optional colored countdown tags on the main calendar and month widget (up to 3 per day).
- **App Icon Badge** — Five badge modes: off, top event countdown, today's event count, pinned-only countdown, or a specific event.
- **Full-Screen Lunar Almanac** — Scrollable daily 黄历 with share export.
- **Tear-Off Calendar** — In-app desk calendar with themes and optional zodiac fortune.
- **Month Share Image** — Render the current month calendar as a shareable long image.
- **Date Interval Calculator** — Gregorian (with optional time) or **lunar wheel mode**; copy or share results. Open from **About → Date Interval Calculator**.
- **20 Home-Screen Widgets** — 4 core + 12 styled + 4 countdown widgets; month/week widgets support tap or double-tap date selection (iOS 17+); StandBy compatible.
- **5 Lock-Screen Widgets** — Quick date info on iOS 16+ lock screens and StandBy.
- **Widget Guide** — Step-by-step gallery and FAQ under **About → Widget Guide**.
- **App Settings iCloud Sync** — Optional sync for theme, layout, and preferences (separate toggle from countdown iCloud).
- **8 App Icons** — Default icon plus **7 alternates**.
- **Dark Mode** — Full light and dark appearance support.
- **Interface Language** — **Follow System**, **简体中文**, or **繁體中文** (About → Language). There is no separate English UI; non-Chinese system locales display Simplified Chinese.
