<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iCalendar (also known as "爱日历"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Summary

**iCalendar does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.** The app operates entirely on your device and requires no account, no internet connection and no cloud services for its core functions.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, phone number or account).
- **No usage data** or analytics are collected.
- **No cookies or tracking** technologies are used.
- **No third-party services** (advertising, analytics, crash reporting) are integrated.

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage. This data is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

| Toggle | What syncs |
|--------|------------|
| **Sync countdown with iCloud** | Countdown events and categories |
| **Sync app settings with iCloud** | Theme, layout, and app preferences (separate from countdown data) |

Countdown **JSON export/import** stays on your device or wherever you save the file — it does not pass through our servers.

## Data Storage

- **Calendar data** and **countdown events** are stored locally on your device.
- **App settings** (preferences, theme) are stored locally using UserDefaults.
- No data is stored on any external server.

## Children's Privacy

iCalendar does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, no special provisions are needed.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

</section>

| 开关 | 同步内容 |

## 联系我们
