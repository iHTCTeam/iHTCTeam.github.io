<section lang="en" markdown="1">

TwinPolarCam is an iOS dual-camera simultaneous recording app. It captures front and back camera inputs at the same time, records them into separate tracks, and composites the main video with a Picture-in-Picture (PiP) overlay after you stop recording — then saves the result directly to your photo library. **Single-hand operation, segmented recording, crash recovery, and rich PiP customization — all in one app.**

## Getting Started

1. Open TwinPolarCam and grant camera, microphone and photo library permissions when prompted.
2. Choose your recording parameters: resolution, frame rate, HDR, stabilization and night mode.
3. Tap the record button to start dual-camera recording. The main view shows one camera while the PiP overlay shows the other.
4. Drag the PiP window to reposition it, or pinch to resize it.
5. Tap the switch button to swap which camera is the main view and which is PiP.
6. When finished, tap stop. The app composites the two tracks and saves the final video to your photo library.

## Features

- **Dual Camera Simultaneous Recording** — Records front and back cameras at the same time into separate tracks.
- **PiP Overlay Compositing** — After stopping, the app composites the main video with a PiP overlay and saves to the photo library.
- **Main / PiP Role Switching** — Swap front or back camera as the main view at any time during recording.
- **Multi-Resolution** — 720p / 1080p / 2K / 4K (available resolutions depend on device hardware).
- **Frame Rate** — 24 fps / 30 fps.
- **HDR** — Off / On / Auto.
- **Video Stabilization** — Off / Standard / Cinematic.
- **Night Mode** — Off / On / Auto.
- **PiP Customization** — Drag to move, pinch to resize. Configurable border width, border color, corner radius, opacity and relative size.
- **Single-Hand Operation** — Start / stop / pause / screenshot / main camera switch / flashlight — all accessible with one hand.
- **Screen Zoom & Presets** — Pinch gesture and control widget for zooming; save and recall **zoom presets** for quick reframing.
- **Preview Pause** — Pause the live preview without stopping an in-progress recording when you need a moment to adjust.
- **Interruption Coordinator** — Monitors audio, capture and app-level events to handle interruptions gracefully.
- **Segmented Recording** — When interrupted (e.g. phone call), the current segment is finalized automatically; recording is resumable afterward.
- **Crash Recovery** — If the app crashes, previously recorded segments are recovered on the next launch.
- **Auto / Manual Export** — Choose automatic export after stopping, or review and export manually.
- **Pending Exports Manager** — Review, retry or discard exports waiting in the queue from the in-app pending-exports screen.
- **Failure Detection** — Errors are categorized and reported to help diagnose issues.
- **In-App UI Languages** — English and Simplified Chinese (简体中文) only. This support site is also available in Traditional Chinese.

1. 開啟太極雙攝，在提示時授予相機、麥克風和相簿權限。
