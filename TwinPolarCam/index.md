TwinPolarCam (太极双摄) is a **privacy-first dual-camera recording app** for **iPhone and iPad**. It captures **front and back cameras simultaneously**, then **composites a main view + Picture-in-Picture (PiP)** and saves to Photos — **100% on device, no account, no cloud upload**.

**At a glance:** dual-camera capture · draggable PiP · pause/resume · serial **export queue** · list/grid Pending manager · **Live Activity (iOS 16.2+)** · recoverable interruptions · emergency background save · in-app guides · tip jar · English / 简体中文 / 繁體中文 · `twinpolarcam://` deep links.

## Getting Started

1. Open TwinPolarCam on a **multi-cam capable** device (typically iPhone XS/XR or later; compatible iPad Pro models).
2. Grant camera / microphone / Photos permissions when prompted (progressive authorization).
3. Adjust main camera / PiP / audio, then tap **Record**.
4. Stop to enqueue export; manage pending jobs in the **Pending** list (list or grid).
5. Optional: enable **Live Activity**, open the in-app **guides**, or use Home Screen shortcuts / `twinpolarcam://`.
