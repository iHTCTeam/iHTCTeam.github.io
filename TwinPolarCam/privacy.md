<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built TwinPolarCam (also known as "太极双摄" / "太極雙攝") as an app for iPhone. This page describes our policies regarding data collection, use and disclosure when you use our app.

## Summary

**TwinPolarCam does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.** All video recording and processing happen entirely on your device. No account is required, and no internet connection is needed.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, phone number or account).
- **No usage data** or analytics are collected.
- **No third-party SDKs** for advertising, analytics or crash reporting are integrated.
- **No cookies or tracking** technologies are used.
- **No data is uploaded** to any server. All recorded video stays on your device until you choose to share it.

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data created by TwinPolarCam stays on your device:

| Data | Storage | User Control |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Recorded video segments | App sandbox (temporary) | Automatically cleaned after export; or delete the app |
| Exported videos | Photos library | Delete from the Photos app |
| App preferences & settings | Local (UserDefaults) | Reset by deleting the app |
| Crash recovery segments | App sandbox | Automatically offered for export on next launch; or delete the app |
| Pending export queue | App sandbox | Managed in Pending Exports; cleared after successful export or manual discard |

## Permissions

TwinPolarCam requests the following device permissions, only when required for recording:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Camera | Record video from front and back cameras simultaneously |
| Microphone | Record audio alongside video |
| Photo Library (Write) | Save exported videos to your photo library |

Permissions are requested only when you first use the app and are never used for any purpose other than recording and saving video. No data from the camera, microphone or photo library is ever transmitted off your device.

## Children's Privacy

TwinPolarCam does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, no special provisions are required.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

</section>

<section lang="zh-CN" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam（"我们"）为 TwinPolarCam（亦称"太极双摄"/"太極雙攝"）这款 iPhone 应用开发并维护本页面，以告知你在使用应用过程中，我们关于数据收集、使用与披露的政策。

## 概要

**太极双摄不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。** 所有视频录制与处理完全在你的设备上完成。无需账号，也无需联网。

## 数据收集

我们不收集任何数据。具体而言：

- **不收集任何个人信息**（无姓名、邮箱、电话或账号）。
- **不收集使用数据**或统计分析。
- **不集成任何第三方 SDK**用于广告、统计或崩溃上报。
- **不使用 Cookie 或追踪**技术。
- **不上传任何数据**到服务器。所有录制的视频都留在你的设备上，直到你选择分享。

## 设备本地存储的数据

太极双摄创建的所有数据都留在你的设备上：

| 数据 | 存储位置 | 用户控制 |
|------|---------|--------------|
| 录制的视频片段 | 应用沙盒（临时）| 导出后自动清理；或删除应用 |
| 导出的视频 | 相册 | 在「照片」应用中删除 |
| 应用偏好与设置 | 本地（UserDefaults）| 删除应用即可重置 |
| 崩溃恢复片段 | 应用沙盒 | 下次启动时自动提示导出；或删除应用 |
| 待导出队列 | 应用沙盒 | 在待导出管理中处理；成功导出或手动丢弃后清除 |

## 权限

太极双摄仅在录制所需时请求以下设备权限：

| 权限 | 用途 |
|------------|---------|
| 相机 | 同时录制前后摄像头视频 |
| 麦克风 | 随视频录制音频 |
| 相册（写入）| 将导出的视频保存到相册 |

权限仅在你首次使用应用时请求，且仅用于录制和保存视频。来自相机、麦克风或相册的数据绝不会从你的设备传出。

## 儿童隐私

太极双摄不会有意收集任何人（包括 13 岁以下儿童）的个人数据。因为我们完全不收集数据，所以无需特别条款。

## 政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更都会更新本页并标注日期。建议你定期回访。

## 联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系：

- **邮箱：** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
