<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iRootWord (爱词根) helps you **learn one root and unlock many words**. **No account required.** Built around **morphology** — not rote memorization — with a **240+ affix dictionary**, **offline word decomposer**, and **Leitner spaced-repetition review**. Browse prefixes, roots, and suffixes by meaning, etymology, or exam category; decompose any English word into affix segments; review with flashcards and quizzes; and track streaks with **Swift Charts**. Stay close to learning with **4 widgets**, **Control Center**, **Siri**, **Shortcuts**, and **Spotlight**. Optional **iCloud sync** — default off. **Fully offline. No ads. No tracking.**

> **Note:** Etymology data is compiled from public open references for study purposes.

## Getting Started

1. Complete the **5-page welcome guide** on first launch (re-open in **Settings → Guides**).
2. Browse **Categories** — semantic themes, etymology languages, affix types, and exam sets (CET-4/6, GRE, IELTS/TOEFL).
3. Tap any affix for detail: Chinese meaning, etymology, variants, memory tips, example words, and morphological breakdown.
4. Open the **Decomposer** — type any English word to see offline affix stripping and root matching with segment-by-segment explanations.
5. Star affixes to **Favorites**; pin important ones for quick access.
6. Start **Review** when cards are due — rate **Again / Hard / Good / Easy**; the Leitner engine schedules your next session.
7. Check **Stats** for streaks, mastery distribution, and weekly study curves.
8. Add **widgets** (home & lock screen), the **Start Review** Control Center control (iOS 18+), or ask **Siri** for today's root.

> Category lists support **list/grid layout** switching; **Today's root** can refresh, and search UX is tightened.

## Features

### Affix Dictionary

- **240+ affixes** — 44 prefixes · 171 roots · 30 suffixes
- **770+ example words** with morphological breakdown
- **46 categories** — 34 semantic themes · 4 etymology languages · 4 types · 4 exam sets
- Detail pages: meaning, etymology, variants, memory tips, citations, example words
- **American & British TTS** pronunciation on every entry
- Trilingual UI (English · 简体中文 · 繁體中文); dictionary content in Simplified with live 简→繁 conversion

### Offline Word Decomposer

- Type any English word — **prefix/suffix stripping + root matching** on-device
- Segment-by-segment explanations; tap matched affixes to jump to dictionary detail
- No network required — algorithm runs against the built-in affix table

### Scientific Review (SRS)

- **Leitner spaced repetition** — box intervals: 0, 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32 days
- **Flashcards** and **multiple-choice quizzes**
- Rate **Again / Hard / Good / Easy**; mastered when reaching the top box
- Due count on home screen, widgets, and review entry

### Learning Stats

- **Study streak** and daily goal tracking
- **Mastery distribution** and **weekly study curve** (Swift Charts)
- Review history and progress at a glance

### Favorites & Search

- Star, pin, drag-sort, and swipe-delete favorites
- Unified search across affixes and example words
- iOS 18+ **dedicated Search tab** on iPhone

### Widgets & System Integration

- **4 widgets** — daily root, hourly root, review progress, favorites glance (home & lock screen)
- **Control Center** (iOS 18+) — **Start Review** control
- **Siri & Shortcuts** — today's root, decompose a word, start review
- **Spotlight** — index all affixes; tap to open detail
- **Home Screen Quick Actions** — up to 4 customizable shortcuts
- **`irootword://` deep links** — see [Shortcut Links](url-scheme)
- **TipKit** tips for discovering widgets and shortcuts (iOS 17+)

### Privacy

- **No account · no registration · no ads · no tracking**
- Dictionary bundled in app — browse, search, decompose, and review offline
- Optional iCloud for favorites and SRS progress (**default off**)
