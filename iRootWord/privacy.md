<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam built iRootWord (爱词根 / 愛詞根). This page describes our data practices.

## Summary

**iRootWord does not require an account and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** The affix dictionary is bundled in the app. Your favorites, SRS progress, and preferences are **stored on your device**; optional **iCloud Sync** can keep them in step across your devices through **your private iCloud** — default **off**. **No ads. No tracking.**

## Data Collection

- **No account** — no registration, email, or phone number required.
- **No analytics or ads** — no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- **No cloud dictionary** — lookups and decompositions never leave your device.

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Affix dictionary (SQLite) | Bundled read-only dictionary |
| Favorites | Affixes you star or pin |
| SRS progress | Leitner box levels, due dates, study log |
| Study streak & daily goal | Learning stats |
| Preferences | Theme, language, TTS, widget settings |
| Widget snapshot | Lightweight data for home-screen widgets |
| Onboarding & tips state | Whether you've seen guides |
| iCloud sync toggle | Local preference for enabling sync |

## iCloud Sync

Optional iCloud sync keeps favorites and SRS progress (study log, streaks) in step across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and visionOS signed in to the same Apple ID. Data syncs through **your private iCloud key-value store** — not iHTCTeam servers. **Disabled by default.**

## Permissions

iRootWord requests no special permissions beyond optional iCloud. No camera, microphone, or location access.

## Open Source

App source code is available under **GPL-3.0** at [github.com/iHTCboy/iRootWord](https://github.com/iHTCboy/iRootWord).

iHTCTeam 开发了爱词根。本页说明我们的数据处理方式。

## 摘要

**爱词根无需账号，不在 iHTCTeam 服务器上收集个人数据。** 词根词缀词典内置在应用中。你的收藏、SRS 进度与偏好**保存在设备上**；可选 **iCloud 同步**可通过**你的私有 iCloud** 在多设备间保持一致——**默认关闭**。**无广告、无追踪。**

## 数据收集

- **无账号**——无需注册、邮箱或手机号。
- **无分析或广告**——不集成第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。
- **无云端词典**——查词与拆词不离开设备。

## 设备本地存储

| 数据 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| 词根词缀词典（SQLite） | 内置只读词典 |
| 收藏 | 你加星标或置顶的词根词缀 |
| SRS 进度 | Leitner 盒子等级、到期日、学习记录 |
| 连续打卡与每日目标 | 学习统计 |
| 偏好设置 | 主题、语言、朗读、小组件设置 |
| 小组件快照 | 主屏小组件的轻量数据 |
| 引导与提示状态 | 是否已看过使用指南 |
| iCloud 同步开关 | 是否启用同步 |

## iCloud 同步

可选 iCloud 同步让登录同一 Apple ID 的 iPhone、iPad、Mac 与 visionOS 间保持收藏与 SRS 进度（学习记录、连续打卡）一致。数据通过**你的私有 iCloud 键值存储**同步——不经过 iHTCTeam 服务器。**默认关闭。**

## 权限

爱词根除可选 iCloud 外不请求特殊权限。不使用相机、麦克风或定位。
