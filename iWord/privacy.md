iHTCTeam built iWord (爱词语 / 愛詞語).

## Summary

**iWord does not require an account and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** All content is in a local SQLite database. Optional **iCloud Sync** for favorites and preferences only — **SRS and learning data stay local**. **No ads. No tracking.**

## Data Stored on Your Device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Entry database | 107,000+ idioms, xiehouyu, riddles, etc. |
| Favorites | Entries you star |
| Browse history | Recent views |
| SRS progress | Review schedules, ratings, streaks |
| Quiz & game records | Scores, wrong-answer book |
| Preferences | Theme, language, TTS settings |
| Tongue-twister recordings | Temporary local audio files |

## iCloud Sync

Optional sync for favorites and preferences through your private iCloud. **Learning progress is not synced** — default off.

## Permissions

| Permission | When | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Microphone | Tongue-twister recording (iOS) | Compare your pronunciation — local temp files only |
| iCloud | Optional sync | Sync favorites and preferences |

## Data Attribution

Content sourced from [jingmo-data](https://github.com/hefengbao/jingmo-data) (MIT) with in-app attribution.

## Contact

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

## 摘要

**爱词语无需账号，不在 iHTCTeam 服务器上收集个人数据。** 全部内容存于本地 SQLite 数据库。可选 **iCloud 同步**仅用于收藏与偏好——**SRS 与学习数据保留在本地**。**无广告、无追踪。**

## 设备本地存储

| 数据 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| 词条数据库 | 10 万+ 成语、歇后语、谜语等 |
| 收藏 | 你加星标的词条 |
| 浏览历史 | 最近查看 |
| SRS 进度 | 复习计划、评分、连续天数 |
| 测验与游戏记录 | 分数、错题本 |
| 偏好设置 | 主题、语言、朗读设置 |

## iCloud 同步

可选通过私有 iCloud 同步收藏与偏好。**学习进度不同步**——默认关闭。

| iCloud | 可选同步 | 同步收藏与偏好 |
