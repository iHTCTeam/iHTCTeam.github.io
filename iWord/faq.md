<section lang="en" markdown="1">

### Is iWord fully offline?

Yes. All **107,000+ entries** are stored in a local SQLite database. Browse, search, review, and play games without internet.

### Do I need an account?

No. **No registration, no login, no account.**

### What content is included?

Six modules: **49,639 idioms**, **14,026 xiehouyu**, **51 tongue twisters**, **964 proverbs**, **490 couplets**, and **42,446 riddles**.

### How does SRS review work?

Rate yourself **0–5** on each card. The SM-2-style engine schedules your next review based on your ratings. Due cards appear on the home screen and in widgets.

### What games are available?

**Idiom quiz** (4-choice), **成语接龙** (chain game), **飞花令** (character search), guess xiehouyu, and guess riddles.

### Why does the app need microphone access?

**Only** for tongue-twister recording on iOS — to compare your pronunciation with TTS. Recordings are temporary local files, not uploaded.

### What does iCloud Sync cover?

Optional sync for **favorites and preferences only**. **SRS progress and learning data stay local** and are not synced.

### What widgets are available?

Daily word, favorites, learning progress, and random — plus lock-screen accessories. iOS 18+ adds Control Center controls for daily word and start review.

### How do I contact support?

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

### 爱词语是完全离线的吗？

是的。全部 **10 万+ 词条**存于本地 SQLite 数据库。浏览、搜索、复习与游戏均无需联网。

### 需要注册账号吗？

不需要。**无需注册、登录或账号。**

### 包含哪些内容？

六大模块：**49,639 条成语**、**14,026 条歇后语**、**51 条绕口令**、**964 条谚语**、**490 副对联**、**42,446 条谜语**。

### SRS 复习怎么工作？

每张卡片自评 **0–5 分**。SM-2 风格引擎根据评分安排下次复习。待复习卡片显示在首页与小组件中。

### 有哪些游戏？

**成语测验**（四选一）、**成语接龙**、**飞花令**、猜歇后语与猜谜语。

### 为什么需要麦克风权限？

**仅**用于 iOS 绕口令录音——与朗读对比发音。录音为本地临时文件，不上传。

### iCloud 同步什么？

可选同步**收藏与偏好**。**SRS 进度与学习数据保留在本地**，不同步。

### 有哪些小组件？

每日一词、收藏、学习进度与随机——含锁屏配件。iOS 18+ 提供控制中心「每日一词」与「开始复习」控件。

### 如何联系支持？

[AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
