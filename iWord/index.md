iWord (爱词语) is a **fully offline Chinese language & culture library** — explore the richness of Chinese expression through **idioms, xiehouyu, tongue twisters, proverbs, couplets, and riddles**. **No account required.** **107,000+ entries** powered by SQLite **FTS5** full-text search: find anything by Chinese, pinyin, or abbreviation. Learn with **SRS spaced-repetition review**, **idiom quizzes**, **成语接龙**, and **飞花令**; listen with **TTS** on every entry; and stay engaged with **daily word**, streaks, and goals. **4 widgets**, **2 Control Center controls**, **Siri**, **Spotlight**, and **`iword://` deep links**. Optional **iCloud** for favorites. **No ads. No tracking.**

> **Note:** Content is sourced from [jingmo-data](https://github.com/hefengbao/jingmo-data) (MIT) with in-app attribution.

## Getting Started

1. Complete the **6-page welcome guide** on first launch (re-open in **Settings → Guides**).
2. Explore **six content modules** from the home screen — idioms, xiehouyu, tongue twisters, proverbs, couplets, riddles.
3. Use **Search** (dedicated tab on iOS 18+) — FTS5 finds entries by Chinese, pinyin, or abbreviations instantly.
4. Tap any entry for rich detail — definitions, pinyin, source, citations, examples, synonyms/antonyms (idioms); flip-to-reveal UI (xiehouyu & riddles).
5. Open **Learn** for **SRS review cards** — rate yourself 0–5; the engine schedules your next review.
6. Play **成语接龙** (chain game), **飞花令**, guess xiehouyu, or guess riddles.
7. Try **tongue twister recording** (iOS) — record yourself and compare with TTS.
8. Add **widgets** and **Control Center** controls; ask **Siri** for today's word or start a review session.
