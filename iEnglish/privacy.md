<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iEnglish (also known as "爱英语" / "初学者必备词汇"). This page describes our policies regarding data collection, use and disclosure when you use our app.

## Summary

**iEnglish does not collect, store or transmit any personal data to iHTCTeam servers.** All vocabulary data, quizzes and playback happen on your device. No account is required, and no internet connection is needed for core features.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (no name, email, phone number or account).
- **No usage data** or analytics are collected.
- **No third-party SDKs** for advertising, analytics or crash reporting are integrated.
- **No cookies or tracking** technologies are used.

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data created by iEnglish stays on your device:

| Data | Storage | User Control |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Vocabulary database (3000 words) | Local (SQLite) | Read-only, bundled with the app |
| Favorites & learning status | Local (App Group) | Remove per word |
| Quiz progress & streak | Local (App Group) | Reset in Settings or by deleting the app |
| Wrong-answer book | Local (App Group) | Clear in Settings |
| Recent browsing | Local (App Group) | Clear on Words tab or in Settings |
| Playlists | Local (UserDefaults) | Delete individual playlists |
| App preferences & settings | Local (UserDefaults) | Reset by deleting the app |

Widgets use an **App Group** container (`group.cc.ihtc.iEnglish`) to share data between the main app and widget extensions. This container stays on your device and is not accessible to any third party.

## Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable **iCloud Sync** in Settings, favorites, learning status, and the wrong-answer book sync across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac devices signed in to the same Apple ID via **iCloud Key-Value storage** (keys under `iCloud.com.iHTCboy.iEnglish`). Data stays in **your private iCloud account** through Apple's infrastructure — not iHTCTeam servers. You can turn sync off anytime; local data remains on each device, and you may optionally remove the copy stored in iCloud.

## In-App Tipping

iEnglish offers optional tips via **StoreKit 2**. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store or have access to any financial information, credit card details or Apple ID data. Tip transactions are governed by Apple's own privacy policy and terms.

## Permissions

iEnglish does not request any special device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photo library or local network.

## Children's Privacy

iEnglish does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, no special provisions are required. The app contains no advertising, no social features and no user-generated content.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)

</section>

在设置中开启 **iCloud 同步** 后，收藏、学习状态与错题本会在登录同一 Apple ID 的 iPhone、iPad、Mac 之间通过 **iCloud 键值存储**（`iCloud.com.iHTCboy.iEnglish` 相关键）同步。数据存于 **你的私有 iCloud 账户**，经 Apple 基础设施传输——不经过 iHTCTeam 服务器。可随时关闭；各设备保留本地数据，并可选择是否清除 iCloud 上的副本。

## 权限

爱英语不请求任何特殊设备权限。不访问你的相机、麦克风、通讯录、位置、相册或本地网络。

爱英语不会有意收集任何人（包括 13 岁以下儿童）的个人数据。因为我们完全不收集数据，所以无需特别条款。应用不包含广告、社交功能或用户生成内容。

## 政策变更

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更都会更新本页并标注日期。建议你定期回访。

## 联系我们
