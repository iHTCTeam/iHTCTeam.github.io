<section lang="en" markdown="1">

iEnglish is an English vocabulary learning app for beginners with **3000 core entry-level words** grouped A–Z. It features native pronunciation, daily quizzes, custom playlists, a reading assistant, and widgets — fully offline on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

## Getting Started

1. Open iEnglish and browse words by letter or switch to grid view.
2. Tap any word to see its detail — Apple Dictionary definition, pronunciation, and Youdao lookup.
3. Use the **Quiz** tab to take a daily 5-question quiz and track your streak.
4. Create **Playlists** for sequential word playback with adjustable loops, intervals, and speed.
5. Try the **Reading Assistant** in **Settings** — paste any English text for read-aloud with pause/resume.
6. Mark words as **Learned** or **Mastered** on the detail page; review all marked words in **Settings → Learning Records**; track overall progress in **Settings → Learning Stats**.
7. Optionally enable **iCloud Sync** in Settings to keep favorites, learning status, and the wrong-answer book in sync across your Apple devices.
8. Add **Widgets** to your home screen (iPhone and iPad only) for a Daily Word, quiz progress, or favorites. Open the same destinations via **`ienglish://`** URLs — see the [URL Scheme Reference](url-scheme).

## Features

- **3000 Core Words** — Entry-level vocabulary with A–Z grouping, list/grid view switching, and an optional A–Z sidebar index (toggle in Settings).
- **Word Detail** — Apple Dictionary (native on iOS, multi-dictionary cards on macOS), Youdao lookup, learning-status menu, and long-image sharing.
- **Repeat Play** — Long-press the speaker (iOS) or right-click **Repeat Play** (macOS) for unlimited-loop pronunciation without changing global voice settings.
- **Inline Play Button** — Optional trailing play control on word list rows (Settings; off by default).
- **In-App Review Prompt** — Optional custom full-screen review guide before the system rating dialog.
- **Learning Progress** — Mark words as Learned or Mastered on the detail page; **Learned Rate** in Settings counts both learned and mastered words. **Learning Records** lists every word you have marked, with filters for learned and mastered.
- **iCloud Sync** — Optional toggle in Settings syncs favorites, learning status, and the wrong-answer book across devices signed in to the same iCloud account.
- **Clear Recent** — Remove your recent-browsing history from the Words tab or Settings.
- **Meaning Language** — Choose Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, or English for word translations independently of the app UI language.
- **Native Pronunciation** — English and Chinese TTS with adjustable loops, intervals, volume, and speed.
- **Reading Assistant** — Paste English text for TTS with pause/resume support (Settings).
- **Daily Quiz** — 5 questions per day, streak counter, wrong-answer book with links to word details, and question tracking.
- **Custom Playlists** — Auto-generate word plans, rename, exclude learned/mastered words, **Play from here**, sequential playback with screen stay-on.
- **Widgets (iOS only)** — Daily Word (word pool: all / letter / category on iOS 17+; shuffle ⇄ on iOS 17+), Category Spotlight, Quiz Progress, Favorites, and Wrong Book for home screen and lock screen. Widget background can follow the app theme gradient (toggle in Settings).
- **15 Theme Presets** — Plus custom color picking for a personalized look.
- **macOS Native** — Keyboard shortcuts (⌘F search, ⌘1–5 tabs, Esc back), inline search, multi-dictionary cards, Mac-style dialogs, default grid layout. **Widgets are not available on macOS.**
- **Home Screen Quick Actions** — Customizable, up to 4 items, sortable (iOS).
- **Deep Link Support** — `ienglish://word?id=`, `ienglish://categories`, `ienglish://quiz`, and more. See the [URL Scheme Reference](url-scheme).
- **Tip Jar** — Optional in-app tips via StoreKit 2 (Apple handles all payments).
- **Dark Mode** — Full light and dark appearance support.
- **Multilingual** — English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese; Words tab icon adapts to app language.

## Widgets

iEnglish offers **5 home-screen widgets** and **4 lock-screen widgets** to keep learning at a glance.

### How to Add

1. Long-press your Home Screen to enter edit mode.
2. Tap the **+** button and search for "iEnglish."
3. Choose a widget, swipe to select the size, and tap **Add Widget**.
4. For Lock Screen widgets, long-press the Lock Screen, tap **Customize**, and add an iEnglish accessory.

### Available Widgets

| Widget | Description | Size |
|--------|-------------|------|
| Daily Word | A new English word each day with translation. iOS 17+: choose word pool (all / letter / category); tap **⇄** to shuffle. | Small, Medium |
| Category Spotlight | Featured words from a category. | Medium |
| Quiz Glance | Today's quiz progress and streak. | Small |
| Favorites Glance | Quick look at your favorite words. | Small |
| Wrong Book Glance | Words you got wrong recently. | Small |
| Lock Daily | Today's word on your Lock Screen. | Lock Screen |
| Lock Quiz | Quiz streak at a glance. | Lock Screen |
| Lock Favorites | Favorite word count. | Lock Screen |
| Lock Wrong Book | Wrong-answer count reminder. | Lock Screen |

爱英语是一款面向初学者的英语词汇学习应用，包含 **3000 个核心入门词汇**，按 A–Z 分组。提供原生发音、每日测验、自定义播放列表、阅读助手和小组件——在 iPhone、iPad 和 Mac 上完全离线运行。

## 快速上手

1. 打开爱英语，按字母浏览词汇或切换为网格视图。
2. 点按任意单词查看详情——Apple 词典释义、发音和有道词典查询。
3. 前往「测验」标签页，完成每日 5 题测验，追踪连续打卡天数。
4. 创建「播放列表」进行顺序词汇播放，可调整循环、间隔和速度。
5. 在 **设置** 中试用「阅读助手」——粘贴任意英文文本进行朗读，支持暂停/继续。
6. 在单词详情页将单词标记为 **已学** 或 **已掌握**；在 **设置 → 学习记录** 查看全部已标记单词；在 **设置 → 学习统计** 查看整体进度。
7. 可在 **设置** 中开启 **iCloud 同步**，在登录同一 iCloud 账户的设备间同步收藏、学习状态与错题本。
8. 添加「小组件」到主屏（仅 iPhone / iPad），查看每日单词、测验进度或收藏。亦可用 **`ienglish://`** URL 打开相同入口——详见 [URL Scheme 说明](url-scheme)。

## 功能特性

- **3000 核心词汇** — 入门级词汇，A–Z 分组，列表/网格视图切换，可选 A–Z 侧边索引（设置中开关）。
- **单词详情** — Apple 词典（iOS 原生，macOS 多词典卡片）、有道词典查询、学习状态菜单、长图分享。
- **重复播放** — 详情页喇叭长按（iOS）或右键「重复播放」（macOS），不改全局语音设置即可无限循环。
- **行内播放按钮** — 可选在单词列表行末显示播放按钮（设置中开关，默认关闭）。
- **应用内评分引导** — 可选自定义全屏引导后再调起系统评分。
- **学习进度** — 在单词详情页标记已学/已掌握；**已学比例** 计入已学 + 已掌握。**学习记录** 列出所有已标记单词，可按已学/已掌握筛选。
- **iCloud 同步** — 设置中可选开启，通过 iCloud 在多台 Apple 设备间同步收藏、学习状态与错题本。
- **清空最近浏览** — 在单词页或设置中清除最近浏览记录。
- **释义语言** — 单词翻译可独立于界面语言，选择简体、繁体或英文释义。
- **原生发音** — 英文和中文 TTS，可调循环、间隔、音量和语速。
- **阅读助手** — 粘贴英文文本进行 TTS 朗读，支持暂停/继续（设置页入口）。
- **每日测验** — 每天 5 题，连续打卡计数器、错题本（可点进单词详情）、答题追踪。
- **自定义播放列表** — 随机生成学习计划、重命名、排除已学/已掌握、**从此处播放**、顺序播放与屏幕常亮。
- **小组件（仅 iOS）** — 每日单词（iOS 17+ 可选词池：全库/字母/分类；**⇄ 随机换词**）、分类精选、测验进度、收藏和错题本，支持主屏和锁屏。小组件背景可跟随 App 主题渐变（设置中开关）。
- **15 套主题预设** — 加上自定义颜色选择，打造个性化外观。
- **macOS 原生** — 键盘快捷键（⌘F 搜索、⌘1–5 切换标签、Esc 返回）、内联搜索、多词典卡片、Mac 风格对话框、默认网格布局。**macOS 不提供小组件。**
- **主屏快捷操作** — 可自定义，最多 4 项，可排序（iOS）。
- **深度链接** — `ienglish://word?id=`、`ienglish://categories`、`ienglish://quiz` 等。详见 [URL Scheme 说明](url-scheme)。
- **打赏** — 通过 StoreKit 2 提供可选打赏（支付由 Apple 处理）。
- **深色模式** — 完整支持浅色与深色外观。
- **多语言** — 英文、简体中文和繁体中文；Words 标签页图标随界面语言切换。
