iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built **GLUT Campus** (also known as "桂林理工大学校园通"). This page explains our policies regarding personal data when you use the app.

## Summary

**GLUT Campus does not require an account and does not collect personal profiles for advertising or analytics.** Core preferences and favorites stay on your device. News content is loaded from **public** university website pages.

## No account

- You do **not** need to register or sign in.
- We do not operate a user account system for this app.

## Data we do not collect

- **No personal profile** for ads or marketing (no name, phone, or account identity required by us).
- **No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.**
- **No cookies** for cross-site tracking inside the app.

## What stays on your device

Stored locally (for example via app storage / UserDefaults / on-device files):

- Appearance preferences (theme, language, accent color, text size, layout)
- **Favorite articles** (separate from temporary caches)
- Temporary **news list / image caches** to improve performance

**Clear Cache** removes temporary article and image caches. It does **not** delete your favorites or most appearance settings.

## Network content

When you browse news or open Campus VR / external links, your device requests content from:

- Public pages on **www.glut.edu.cn** (and related public school resources)
- Third-party hosts required for specific features you open (for example Campus VR or WeChat article pages)

Those servers may see standard network request metadata (as with any browser or app). We do not operate a separate analytics backend for this app.

## Photos permission

If you choose to **save** a QR code or article image to Photos, iOS / macOS may ask for photo library add permission. Images are saved only when you initiate the action.

## In-App Purchases

Optional **Tip Developer** purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details. Tipping never unlocks or restricts features.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children for our own databases. Since we do not run an account or ad tracking system, no special child-account flow is required.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact Us

- **Email:** [iHTCdevelop@gmail.com](mailto:iHTCdevelop@gmail.com)
