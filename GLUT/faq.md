<section lang="en" markdown="1">

### Do I need an account?

No. GLUT Campus never asks you to register or sign in. Open the app and start reading.

### Is this the official university admin / academic system?

No. This is an independent campus **news and resources** companion. It does **not** include academic-system login, grades, or course selection. Use official school channels for those services.

### Where does the news come from?

Articles and lists are loaded from **public pages** on the university website ([www.glut.edu.cn](https://www.glut.edu.cn)). The app does not scrape private or login-walled content.

### Why won’t some articles open in the app?

The official site layout can change. Use **Open in Browser** from the article menu, or pull to refresh and try again later.

### Are lists always live?

Recent lists are **cached on your device** for faster browsing (and weak networks). Pull to refresh for the latest; a successful refresh updates the cache.

### How do I change article text size?

**Settings → Appearance → Text Size**, or open the text-size panel from an article’s **More** menu. Range: 50%–300%.

### How do favorites work? Does clearing cache delete them?

Save articles from the list or detail **More** menu. Favorites are stored separately from temporary caches — **Clear Cache never removes favorites**.

### How do I share a full-page screenshot?

Open an article → **More** → **Share Full-Page Screenshot**. The app stitches a long image (with an App Store footer) you can send to classmates.

### Where is Campus VR?

**Settings → GLUT → Campus VR**. A stable network helps. On iPhone/iPad it opens full screen; on Mac it opens in a large sheet.

### How do I change the Home Screen icon?

iPhone / iPad only: **Settings → Appearance → App Icon**. Choose from 13 icons (default, 70th-anniversary, and seal styles). iOS may show a system confirmation.

### What languages are supported?

**Follow System**, **Simplified Chinese**, **Traditional Chinese**, or **English**. Changes apply instantly — no restart.

### What is Tip Developer?

An optional StoreKit tip. It is completely voluntary and **does not unlock or lock any features**. You can cancel before confirming payment.

### Still need help?

Email [iHTCdevelop@gmail.com](mailto:iHTCdevelop@gmail.com) — we typically reply within 48 hours.

<section lang="zh-CN" markdown="1">

### 需要注册或登录吗？

不需要。校园通从不要求注册或登录，打开即可使用。

### 这是学校官方教务 / 管理系统吗？

不是。这是独立的校园**资讯与资料**伴侣，**不含**教务登录、成绩或选课。相关服务请使用学校官方渠道。

### 新闻从哪里来？

列表与正文来自学校官网（[www.glut.edu.cn](https://www.glut.edu.cn)）的**公开页面**。应用不会抓取需要登录的私有内容。

### 为什么有的文章打不开正文？

官网结构偶有调整，解析可能失败。请在文章菜单选择 **用浏览器打开**，或稍后下拉刷新再试。

### 列表是实时的吗？

近期列表会**缓存在本机**，便于弱网浏览。下拉刷新可拉取最新；刷新成功后会更新本地缓存。

### 如何调整正文字号？

**设置 → 外观 → 正文字号**，或在文章 **更多** 菜单打开字号面板。范围：50%–300%。

### 收藏怎么用？清理缓存会丢收藏吗？

在列表或详情的 **更多** 菜单收藏。收藏与临时缓存分开保存——**清理缓存不会删除收藏**。

### 如何分享全文长图？

打开文章 → **更多** → **分享全文截图**。应用会拼接长图（含 App Store 页脚），方便发给同学。

### 校园 VR 在哪里？

**设置 → 桂工 → 校园 VR**。请保持网络畅通。iPhone / iPad 为全屏；Mac 为大尺寸面板。

### 如何更换主屏幕图标？

仅 iPhone / iPad：**设置 → 外观 → 应用图标**。共 13 套（默认、七秩校庆、校徽印记）。系统可能会提示「图标已更改」。

### 支持哪些语言？

**跟随系统**、**简体中文**、**繁体中文** 或 **English**。切换即时生效，无需重启。

### 「打赏开发者」是什么？

可选的 StoreKit 打赏，完全自愿，**不解锁也不限制任何功能**。确认支付前可随时取消。

### 还有问题？

发信至 [iHTCdevelop@gmail.com](mailto:iHTCdevelop@gmail.com)，通常 48 小时内回复。
