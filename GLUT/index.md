**GLUT Campus** (桂林理工大学校园通) is the pocket companion for **Guilin University of Technology**. For ten years it has helped students and teachers **read campus news, catch notices, explore academic updates, and find school resources** — now fully rebuilt in SwiftUI for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

**At a glance:** News · Study · Campus · Settings · headline carousels · favorites · text size · long-screenshot sharing · Campus VR · 6 accent colors · **13 app icons** · English / 简体 / 繁體 · **no account, no ads, no tracking**.

> Content comes from **public pages** on [www.glut.edu.cn](https://www.glut.edu.cn). The app is an independent alumni project and is **not** an official university administrative system — there is **no academic-system login**.

## Why download

- **Stay informed in seconds** — Guilin University headlines, notices, media coverage, and study updates in clear categories, with pull-to-refresh.
- **Read the way you like** — adjustable article text size (50%–300%), favorites that survive cache clears, share links or full-page long screenshots.
- **Find campus info fast** — nested guides for school intro and faculties, with search and list / grid / card layouts.
- **Explore campus in VR** — immersive tour from Settings, plus quick links to the official site, Weibo, WeChat, and the president’s mailbox.
- **Make it yours** — light / dark / system theme, six accent colors, and thirteen Home Screen icons (including 70th-anniversary styles on iOS).
- **Works everywhere you study** — the same clear four-tab experience on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

## Getting Started

1. Open GLUT Campus — **no sign-in**. The short feature tour appears on first launch (replay anytime in **Settings → Feature Tour**).
2. On the **News** tab, switch among campus headlines, notices, general news, and media coverage; pull to refresh.
3. Open the **Study** tab for academic events, campus briefs, media hub, and integrity updates.
4. Tap an article to read; use **More** to change text size, save a favorite, share a link, or create a long screenshot.
5. Browse the **Campus** tab for local resources; search by title or blurb; switch list / grid / cards.
6. In **Settings**, customize appearance, open **Campus VR**, follow official channels, or tip the developer (optional).

- **Campus headlines** with a featured carousel from the university home page

### Look & feel

- **13 app icons** on iPhone and iPad (default + 70th anniversary + seal styles)
- UI in **English · Simplified Chinese · Traditional Chinese**

### Privacy & platforms

- **No registration, no account, no third-party analytics or ads**
- Favorites, preferences, and caches stay on your device
- Built for **iPhone, iPad, and Mac** (iOS 17+ / macOS 14+)

### Anniversary notes
