iHTCTeam ("we", "us" or "our") built iApp (also known as "爱应用" / "愛應用"). This page describes our policies regarding data collection, use and disclosure when you use our app.

## Summary

**iApp does not require an account and does not collect personal data on iHTCTeam servers.** The app reads **public App Store data** over the internet. Your favorites, filters, rank history, search history, and subscription settings are **saved on your device**; optional **iCloud Sync** can sync favorites, subscriptions, filter presets, and search-region preferences through **iCloud**. On iOS 17+, favorites are also shared with Home Screen widgets on your device.

## Data Collection

We do not operate a backend that collects your personal information. Specifically:

- **No account** — No registration, email, or phone number is required.
- **No analytics or ads** — No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are integrated.
- **No content access** — We cannot see which apps you browse, search for, or favorite.

## How the App Works

iApp retrieves **public** app information from the App Store over the internet:

- Rankings, search results, and detail pages require network access.
- Data is provided by Apple; iApp does not modify store listings.
- Opening the official App Store or in-app product page uses Apple's system services.

## iCloud Sync

iCloud sync uses **separate toggles** in **Settings → Data**:

- **Favorites** — your saved apps (per App ID and region), groups, notes, and pins; merged by timestamp so deletions are respected
- **Developer Favorites** — saved developers (per artist + region); **separate toggle, default off**; timestamp-based merge
- **Subscriptions** — your watch list (optional)
- **Filter presets** — rank-tracking presets (optional)
- **Search region & quick regions** — search defaults and quick-region chips (optional)

Data syncs across iPhone, iPad, and Mac devices signed in to the same Apple ID through **iCloud**. Data stays in **your private iCloud account** — not on iHTCTeam servers.

On **iOS 17+**, favorites needed by widgets are also kept on your device for the widget to read. This data does not leave your device except through **iCloud** when sync is enabled.

## Permissions

iApp requests the following permissions only when needed:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Network | Load rankings, search, and app details from the App Store |
| Photo Library (Add) | Save share cards, screenshots, or **App Preview videos with audio** you choose to export |

## Children's Privacy

iApp does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. Since iHTCTeam does not operate a data-collection backend, no special provisions are required beyond Apple's platform policies.

## Disclaimer

iApp is **not affiliated with Apple Inc.** and does not provide app download or installation services. All store data comes from public App Store sources; refer to the official App Store for purchases and downloads.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

- **Email:** [AppleOSer@gmail.com](mailto:AppleOSer@gmail.com)
